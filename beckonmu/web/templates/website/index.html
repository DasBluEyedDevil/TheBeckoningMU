Beneath the marble columns and sunlit facades of Athens lies a city the mortals will never know. Here, among the ruins of civilizations that rose and fell like the tides of the Aegean, the Kindred hold court in shadows older than memory. The Beckoning is a text-based multiplayer chronicle set in the cradle of Western civilization, where ancient blood feuds simmer beneath the veneer of modern Greek nightlife.

Built upon the Vampire: The Masquerade 5th Edition ruleset, every mechanical detail has been faithfully rendered: Hunger dice claw at your restraint, Resonance colors the vitae you consume, and the Traditions of the Camarilla are enforced not by game masters alone, but by the merciless architecture of the World of Darkness itself. Forge alliances in the Elysium of the Odeon, trade secrets in the neon-lit bars of Psyrri, or hunt along the moonlit shores of Piraeus.

This is not a game of heroes. This is a chronicle of monsters — and the humanity they cling to as it slips away, one Rouse check at a time.
