|W|                     DICE ROLLING COMMANDS                        |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Commands for rolling V5 dice pools.

|WBASIC ROLLING:|n

|yroll <pool> [<hunger>] [vs <difficulty>]|n
|yr <pool> [<hunger>] [vs <difficulty>]|n
  Roll a V5 dice pool with optional Hunger dice.

|yExamples:|n
  roll 7                         # Roll 7 dice
  roll 5 2 vs 3                  # 5 dice, Hunger 2, difficulty 3
  roll 6 vs 2                    # 6 dice vs difficulty 2

|ySwitches:|n
  /willpower - Offer Willpower reroll on failure
  /secret - Only show result to you (no room broadcast)

|yExamples:|n
  roll/willpower 4 3             # With Willpower option
  roll/secret 6 vs 2             # Secret roll

|WDISCIPLINE POWER ROLLS:|n

|ypower <power name> [vs <difficulty>]|n
|ydiscipline <power name> [vs <difficulty>]|n
  Roll a discipline power automatically.

|yExamples:|n
  power Corrosive Vitae          # Auto-calculate pool
  power Corrosive Vitae vs 3     # vs difficulty 3
  power/norouse Heightened Senses # Skip Rouse check

The system automatically:
  - Looks up the discipline power
  - Calculates dice pool from your traits
  - Applies Blood Potency bonuses
  - Performs Rouse check (unless /norouse)
  - Rolls with your current Hunger

|WROUSE CHECKS:|n

|yrouse [<reason>]|n
  Perform a Rouse check.

|yExamples:|n
  rouse                          # Basic Rouse check
  rouse Blood Surge              # Rouse with reason

Rouse checks are made when using vampiric powers, healing,
or performing blood-powered actions. On failure (1-5),
Hunger increases by 1.

|WDICE MECHANICS REFERENCE:|n

|yshowdice|n
|yshowdice hunger|n
|yshowdice criticals|n
|yshowdice bestial|n
  Display V5 dice mechanics reference.

Shows information about:
  - Success thresholds
  - Critical wins
  - Hunger dice mechanics
  - Messy Criticals
  - Bestial Failures

|WV5 DICE RULES:|n

|ySuccess:|n
  - Each die is d10 (1-10)
  - |g6-9|n = 1 success
  - |y10|n = 2 successes (critical)
  - 1-5 = no success

|yCritical Win:|n
  - Pair of 10s = Critical Success
  - Each pair adds 4 total successes

|yHunger Dice:|n
  - Replace regular dice (not added)
  - Number equals current Hunger level
  - |r1|n on Hunger die can cause Bestial Failure
  - |r10|n on Hunger die can cause Messy Critical

|yMessy Critical:|n
  - Critical win with at least one Hunger 10
  - You succeed, but the Beast influences outcome
  - Gain a Stain on Humanity

|yBestial Failure:|n
  - Failed roll with Hunger dice showing 1s
  - The Beast seizes control
  - Clan Compulsion may activate

|yWillpower Reroll:|n
  - Spend 1 Willpower to reroll up to 3 failed dice
  - Cannot reroll Hunger dice
