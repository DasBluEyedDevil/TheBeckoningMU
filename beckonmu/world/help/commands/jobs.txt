|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                          JOBS SYSTEM                              |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

The Jobs system allows players to submit requests, questions, and issues to
staff, and enables staff to track and manage these requests efficiently.

|WBASIC COMMANDS:|n

|yjob/create <bucket>=<title>|n
  - Submit a new job to staff
  - Buckets: char, hunt, build, code, request, bug
  - Example: job/create char=Request approval for my background

|yjobs|n
  - View all your submitted jobs and their status

|yjob <number>|n
  - Read the full details of a job
  - Example: job 42

|yjob/comment <number>=<text>|n
  - Add a comment to one of your jobs
  - Staff will be notified of your comment

|WJOB STATUSES:|n

|gOPEN|n - Job is active and awaiting staff attention
|yCLOSED|n - Job has been resolved and closed by staff
|yON HOLD|n - Job is temporarily paused (waiting for player response)

|WJOB BUCKETS:|n

|ychar|n - Character creation, backgrounds, approvals
|yhunt|n - Request staff-run hunt scenes
|ybuild|n - Building requests, room descriptions
|ycode|n - Bug reports related to code/commands
|yrequest|n - General requests and questions
|ybug|n - Non-code bugs or issues

|WCHARACTER APPROVAL WORKFLOW:|n

When you create a character via web or in-game chargen:
1. A job is automatically created in the |ychar|n bucket
2. Staff will review your character sheet and background
3. Staff may comment with questions or requested changes
4. Once approved, the job is closed and you can play

|WSTAFF COMMANDS:|n (Staff only)

|yjobs|n - View all jobs in the system (staff see all jobs)
|yjob/claim <number>|n - Claim a job to work on it
|yjob/assign <number>=<staffer>|n - Assign a job to another staffer
|yjob/done <number>=<resolution>|n - Close a job with notes
|yjob/reopen <number>|n - Reopen a closed job

|WBUCKET MANAGEMENT:|n (Admin only)

|ybucket/create <name>=<description>|n - Create a new job bucket
|ybucket/list|n - List all available buckets
|ybucket/delete <name>|n - Delete a bucket

|WTIPS:|n

- Be specific in job titles - helps staff prioritize
- Include all relevant information in the initial submission
- Check back regularly for staff comments
- Mark jobs as read after viewing staff responses
- Use the appropriate bucket for faster handling

|WEXAMPLES:|n

|yRequest character approval:|n
  job/create char=Background approval for Marcus Valerius

|yRequest a hunt scene:|n
  job/create hunt=Looking for a Choleric resonance hunt in the warehouse district

|yReport a bug:|n
  job/create bug=+sheet command not showing my disciplines correctly

|yGeneral question:|n
  job/create request=Question about blood bond mechanics
