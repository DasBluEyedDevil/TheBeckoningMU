Commands for viewing your character's statistics and status.

|WFULL CHARACTER SHEET:|n

|y+sheet|n
|ysheet|n
  Display your comprehensive character sheet.

Shows all V5 information including:
  - Vampire vitals (generation, blood potency, hunger, humanity)
  - Clan information (bane, compulsion)
  - Attributes (Physical, Social, Mental)
  - Skills (with specialties)
  - Disciplines (with known powers)
  - Backgrounds, Merits, and Flaws
  - Humanity (convictions, touchstones, stains)
  - Health, Willpower, and Experience

The full sheet is formatted with gothic V:tM theming for easy
reading of all your character's capabilities.

|WQUICK STATUS:|n

|y+st|n
|yst|n
|ystatus|n
  Display abbreviated character stats.

Shows vital statistics in a compact one-line format:
  - Name
  - Hunger level
  - Health status
  - Willpower status
  - Humanity rating

Perfect for quick status checks during play without scrolling
through the full sheet.

|yExample Output:|n
  John | Hunger: 2 | Health: OOOOO | Willpower: 4/5 | Humanity: 7
