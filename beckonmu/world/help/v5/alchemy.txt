|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                     THIN-BLOOD ALCHEMY                            |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Thin-Blood Alchemy is a unique discipline available only to
thin-blooded vampires. It allows crafting of alchemical formulae
that mimic discipline powers.

|WHOW IT WORKS:|n

1. |yLearn Formulae:|n
   - Gain Thin-Blood Alchemy dots (1-5)
   - Each level unlocks new formulae

2. |yGather Ingredients:|n
   - Vampire blood (your own or others')
   - Mundane ingredients (quicksilver, ash, herbs, etc.)
   - Rare components for advanced formulae

3. |yCraft Formula:|n
   - Roll Intelligence + Thin-Blood Alchemy vs Difficulty
   - Success: Formula created (single use)
   - Failure: Ingredients consumed, no formula

4. |yUse Formula:|n
   - Activate the crafted formula
   - Effect lasts for duration
   - |yNo Rouse check required!|n

|WLEVEL 1 FORMULAE:|n

|yFar Reach|n (Difficulty 2)
  - Telekinesis to pull/push objects
  - Range: 5 meters
  - Ingredients: vampire blood, quicksilver

|yHaze|n (Difficulty 2)
  - Cloud observers' minds, make them forget
  - Like Obfuscate lite
  - Ingredients: vampire blood, alcohol

|yEnvelop|n (Difficulty 2)
  - Wrap in shadows, harder to see
  - Stealth bonus
  - Ingredients: vampire blood, ash

|WLEVEL 2 FORMULAE:|n

|yCounterfeit|n (Difficulty 3)
  - Create duplicate of small object
  - Lasts one night
  - Ingredients: vampire blood, clay, piece of original

|yDefractionate|n (Difficulty 3)
  - Split consciousness, perceive multiple places
  - Like Auspex scrying
  - Ingredients: vampire blood, mirror shards

|WHIGHER LEVELS:|n

Level 3-5 formulae available with advancement.
See |y+alchemy|n in-game for complete list.

|WADVANTAGES:|n
  - |yNo Rouse checks|n (Blood Potency 0 benefit)
  - Versatile effects
  - Can share with others
  - Unique powers unavailable to other vampires

|WDISADVANTAGES:|n
  - Single use (must re-craft)
  - Requires ingredients
  - Craft roll can fail
  - Effects weaker than true disciplines

|WCOMMANDS:|n

|y+alchemy|n
  View all known formulae

|y+alchemy/craft <formula name>|n
  Craft an alchemical formula
  Example: |y+alchemy/craft far reach|n

|y+alchemy/use <formula name>|n
  Activate a crafted formula
  Example: |y+alchemy/use far reach|n

|y+alchemy/ingredients|n
  View your ingredient inventory
