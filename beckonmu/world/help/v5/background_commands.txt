|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                  BACKGROUND COMMANDS                             |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Commands for viewing and using your Background advantages.
Backgrounds represent your character's social connections,
resources, and external assets.

|WVIEWING BACKGROUNDS:|n

|y+background|n
|ybackgrounds|n
|ybg|n
  List all your backgrounds with their current levels and benefits.

|y+background <name>|n
  View detailed information about a specific background.

|yExamples:|n
  +background                     # List all backgrounds
  +background contacts            # View Contacts details
  +background resources           # View Resources details

|WUSING BACKGROUNDS:|n

|y+background/use <name>=<task description>|n
  Use a background to gain bonus dice for a task.

|yExamples:|n
  +background/use contacts=investigate the gang
  +background/use allies=get police records
  +background/use influence=sway city council vote

|yEffects:|n
  - Grants bonus dice based on background level
  - May consume one of your background's uses per session
  - ST may require a roll to see if contacts come through

|WSPECIAL BACKGROUND COMMANDS:|n

|y+background/herd|n
  Feed from your Herd background.
  Reduces Hunger based on Herd level.
  Safer than hunting on the streets.

|y+background/resources <item>=<rating>|n
  Acquire an item using your Resources background.
  Rating indicates item cost (1-5).
  Can only acquire items at or below your Resources level.

|yExamples:|n
  +background/herd                # Feed from herd
  +background/resources pistol=2  # Buy a pistol (rating 2)
  +background/resources car=3     # Acquire a car (rating 3)

|WBACKGROUND TYPES:|n

|yAllies|n - Mortal friends who help when asked
|yContacts|n - Information sources in various fields
|yFame|n - Public recognition and celebrity
|yHaven|n - Safe resting place quality
|yHerd|n - Reliable feeding sources
|yInfluence|n - Political or social pull
|yMask|n - Alternate mortal identity
|yMentor|n - Elder vampire guidance
|yResources|n - Wealth and financial access
|yRetainers|n - Loyal servants (may be ghouls)
|yStatus|n - Standing within Kindred society

|WUSES PER SESSION:|n

Most backgrounds have limited uses per game session:

  |yLevel 1-2:|n 1 use per session
  |yLevel 3-4:|n 2 uses per session
  |yLevel 5:|n 3 uses per session

Uses reset at the start of each new session (or when ST calls
for a reset with +background/reset).

Some backgrounds (like Haven, Resources for small purchases)
may have unlimited uses at ST discretion.

|WSTAFF COMMANDS:|n

|y+background/reset|n
  Reset all background uses for a new session. (Staff only)

|WTIPS:|n

- Describe HOW you're using the background for better results
- Backgrounds work best when they fit the narrative
- ST may modify results based on circumstances
- Overusing contacts may strain relationships

|yExample Good Usage:|n
  +background/use contacts=I call my source at the
  police department to check for missing persons matching
  the victim's description.
