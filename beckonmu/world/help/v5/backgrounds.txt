|W|                          BACKGROUNDS                              |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Backgrounds represent resources, connections, and advantages your
character possesses. Unlike Attributes and Skills, Backgrounds
provide |ymechanical benefits|n that can be used in play.

|WBACKGROUND LIST:|n

|yAllies|n (dots 1-5)
  Mortal or supernatural allies who can provide aid
  |wBenefit:|n Can call for help. +[dots] to Social rolls when relevant
  |wUses:|n [dots] times per session

|yContacts|n (dots 1-5)
  Information sources in various areas
  |wBenefit:|n +[dots] to Investigation when using contacts
  |wUses:|n [dots Ã— 2] times per session

|yFame|n (dots 1-5)
  Recognition in mortal society
  |wBenefit:|n +[dots] to Social rolls with those who recognize you
  |wUses:|n Unlimited (passive)

|yHaven|n (dots 1-5)
  Quality and security of your haven/lair
  |wBenefit:|n Security rating +[dots] to defend against intrusion
  |wUses:|n Passive

|yHerd|n (dots 1-5)
  Regular feeding sources
  |wBenefit:|n Reduce Hunger by [dots] per week without hunting (no risk)
  |wUses:|n Once per week

|yInfluence|n (dots 1-5)
  Sway over mortal institutions
  |wBenefit:|n +[dots] to Leadership/Politics in domain
  |wUses:|n [dots] times per session

|yMask|n (dots 1-5)
  Strength of your mortal identity/cover
  |wBenefit:|n +[dots] to maintain Masquerade and resist investigation
  |wUses:|n Passive

|yResources|n (dots 1-5)
  Wealth and material assets
  |wBenefit:|n Can acquire items of [dots] rating or less
  |wUses:|n [dots] times per session

|yRetainers|n (dots 1-5)
  Loyal servants, ghouls, or employees
  |wBenefit:|n [dots] servants who can perform tasks
  |wUses:|n Unlimited (they're always there)

|yStatus|n (dots 1-5)
  Standing in vampire society
  |wBenefit:|n +[dots] to Social rolls with Kindred
  |wUses:|n Unlimited (passive)

|WUSING BACKGROUNDS:|n

Most backgrounds can be actively used for mechanical benefits:

|y+background|n
  View all your backgrounds

|y+background <name>|n
  View details of a specific background
  Example: |y+background contacts|n

|y+background/use <name>=<task>|n
  Use background for a task
  Example: |y+background/use contacts=investigate gang activity|n

|y+background/herd|n
  Feed from your Herd (reduces Hunger safely)

|y+background/resources <item>=<rating>|n
  Acquire an item using Resources
  Example: |y+background/resources pistol=2|n

|WBACKGROUND RATINGS:|n

|y1 dot:|n  Minor benefit (small apartment, one good contact)
|y2 dots:|n Useful benefit (nice house, several contacts)
|y3 dots:|n Significant benefit (mansion, network of contacts)
|y4 dots:|n Major benefit (estate, influential connections)
|y5 dots:|n Exceptional benefit (multiple properties, powerful allies)

|WIMPORTANT NOTES:|n

- Most backgrounds have |ylimited uses per session|n
- Uses reset at start of each game session
- Staff may award additional uses for good roleplay
- Backgrounds can be increased with XP
- Some backgrounds (Status, Fame) are always "on"
