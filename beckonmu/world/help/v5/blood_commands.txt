|W|                    BLOOD SYSTEM COMMANDS                         |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Commands for managing vampire blood mechanics including feeding,
Blood Surge, and Hunger tracking.

|WFEEDING:|n

|yfeed <target> [<resonance>]|n
  Feed on a mortal to reduce Hunger.

|yfeed/slake <target>|n
  Feed until Hunger reaches 0 (dangerous, multiple rolls).

|yExamples:|n
  feed mortal                    # Hunt a generic mortal
  feed mortal choleric           # Hunt for choleric resonance
  feed/slake victim              # Drain completely (risky)

|yResonances:|n
  - |ycholeric|n - Anger, passion, aggression
  - |ymelancholic|n - Sadness, introspection, artists
  - |yphlegmatic|n - Calm, apathy, laziness
  - |ysanguine|n - Joy, pleasure, desire

Feeding requires a roll based on your Predator Type. On success:
  - Hunger is reduced (1-3 based on margin of success)
  - Your resonance is set to the victim's emotional state
  - |[RMessy Criticals|n may draw attention or leave evidence

|WBLOOD SURGE:|n

|ybloodsurge <attribute or physical skill>|n
|ysurge <attribute or physical skill>|n
  Surge your blood to enhance a trait temporarily.

|yExamples:|n
  bloodsurge strength            # Boost Strength
  surge brawl                    # Boost Brawl
  bloodsurge dexterity           # Boost Dexterity

|yEffects:|n
  - Adds dice equal to your |WBlood Potency|n
  - Lasts for one scene (1 hour)
  - Requires a |WRouse check|n (may increase Hunger)

|yValid Attributes:|n
  Strength, Dexterity, Stamina, Charisma, Manipulation,
  Composure, Intelligence, Wits, Resolve

|yValid Physical Skills:|n
  Athletics, Brawl, Craft, Drive, Firearms, Larceny,
  Melee, Stealth, Survival

|WHUNGER STATUS:|n

|yhunger|n
  View your current Hunger level and blood status.

|yDisplays:|n
  - Current Hunger level (0-5)
  - Visual Hunger bar
  - Hunger effects
  - Current resonance (if any)
  - Blood Surge status (if active)

|WHUNGER EFFECTS:|n

|yHunger 0:|n Well-fed and sated
|yHunger 1-2:|n Minor cravings
|yHunger 3:|n Moderate hunger, need to feed soon
|yHunger 4:|n Severe hunger, the Beast stirs
|yHunger 5:|n |[RRAVENOUS|n - Cannot use most discipline powers

At Hunger 5, your dice pool replaces ALL normal dice with Hunger
dice, making Bestial Failures much more likely.

|WHUNGER DICE MECHANICS:|n

When rolling, Hunger dice replace normal dice equal to your
Hunger level. Hunger dice can cause:

  - |[RMessy Critical|n - Success with a Hunger 10 in a critical
    Your success is violent, obvious, or dramatic

  - |[RBestial Failure|n - Failure with any Hunger dice
    Your clan Compulsion activates, the Beast takes control
