|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                    BOON SYSTEM COMMANDS                          |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Commands for managing boons (political favors and debts) in
Kindred society. See |yhelp boons|n for conceptual overview.

|WVIEWING BOONS:|n

|y+boon|n
|yboons|n
  View your complete boon ledger summary.

Shows:
  - Boons you are owed (credits)
  - Boons you owe (debts)
  - Net political position

|y+boon <character>|n
  View boons between you and a specific character.

|y+boon/pending|n
  View boons requiring your action.

|yExamples:|n
  +boon
  +boon Marcus
  +boon/pending

|WOFFERING A BOON:|n

|y+boongive <character> <type> = <description>|n
  Offer a boon to someone (you become the debtor).

|yBoon Types:|n
  - |ytrivial|n - Small favor, minor information
  - |yminor|n - Standard favor, useful service
  - |ymajor|n - Substantial favor, significant risk
  - |yblood|n - Blood-related service (rare)
  - |ylife|n - Life-saving debt (most serious)

|yExamples:|n
  +boongive Marcus minor = Saved me from a hunter
  +boongive Elena major = She covered up my Masquerade breach
  +boongive John trivial = Introduced me to the Primogen

The recipient must accept the boon for it to become official.

|WACCEPTING OR DECLINING:|n

|y+boonaccept <boon #>|n
  Accept a boon that has been offered to you.

|y+boondecline <boon #>|n
|y+boondecline <boon #> = <reason>|n
  Decline a boon offer (optionally with reason).

|yExamples:|n
  +boonaccept 42
  +boondecline 43 = I do not wish to be owed this debt.

|WCALLING IN A BOON:|n

|y+booncall <boon #> = <description>|n
  Call in a boon that is owed to you.

The description explains what favor you're requesting.
The debtor will be notified.

|yExamples:|n
  +booncall 42 = I need you to vote for Marcus at the Primogen meeting.
  +booncall 15 = Provide me an alibi for last night.

|yNote:|n The request should be proportional to the boon level.
Calling in a Life Boon for a trivial favor is poor form.

|WFULFILLING A BOON:|n

|y+boonfulfill <boon #> = <description>|n
  Mark a called boon as fulfilled.

Used by the debtor after completing the requested favor.

|yExamples:|n
  +boonfulfill 42 = Voted for Marcus as requested.
  +boonfulfill 15 = Told the Sheriff you were with me all night.

|WBOON WORKFLOW:|n

1. |yService Performed|n
   Someone helps you, creating an obligation.

2. |yBoon Offered|n
   You use |y+boongive|n to acknowledge the debt.

3. |yBoon Accepted|n
   The creditor uses |y+boonaccept|n to formalize it.

4. |yHarpy Acknowledgment|n (optional)
   For major boons, the Harpy may officially record it.

5. |yBoon Called|n
   When the creditor needs a favor, they use |y+booncall|n.

6. |yBoon Fulfilled|n
   After completing the favor, debtor uses |y+boonfulfill|n.

|WSTAFF/HARPY COMMANDS:|n

|y+boonadmin/acknowledge <boon #>|n
  Officially acknowledge a boon (Harpy function).

|y+boonadmin/cancel <boon #> = <reason>|n
  Cancel a boon.

|y+boonadmin/list|n
  List all public boons.

|y+boonadmin/refresh <character>|n
  Refresh a character's boon ledger calculations.

|WBOON STATUS MEANINGS:|n

|yPending|n - Offered but not yet accepted
|yActive|n - Accepted and waiting to be called
|yCalled|n - Creditor has requested the favor
|yFulfilled|n - Debt has been repaid
|yCancelled|n - Boon was cancelled (rare)
|yDisputed|n - Parties disagree (Harpy arbitration needed)

|WTIPS:|n

- Keep descriptions clear and specific
- Harpy acknowledgment adds legitimacy to major boons
- Trading boons requires both parties to agree
- Life Boons should be offered rarely and never refused
- Use |y+boon/pending|n regularly to check obligations
