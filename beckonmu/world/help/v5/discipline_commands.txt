|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                 DISCIPLINE POWER COMMANDS                        |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Commands for viewing and activating your vampire discipline powers.

|WVIEWING DISCIPLINES:|n

|y+disciplines|n
|y+disc|n
  View all your known disciplines and powers.

|y+disciplines <discipline name>|n
|y+disc <discipline name>|n
  View details of a specific discipline.

|yExamples:|n
  +disciplines                   # Show all disciplines
  +disciplines animalism         # Show Animalism powers
  +disc celerity                 # Show Celerity powers

|WACTIVATING POWERS:|n

|y+power <discipline>/<power name>|n
|y+activate <discipline>/<power name>|n
|y+use <discipline>/<power name>|n
  Activate a discipline power.

|yExamples:|n
  +power animalism/bond famulus
  +power celerity/cat's grace
  +activate dominate/compel
  +use presence/awe

Activating a power will:
  1. Verify you have the required discipline level
  2. Perform a |WRouse check|n if required
  3. Apply any resonance bonuses
  4. Show the dice pool for contested powers
  5. Apply effects (viewable with +effects)

|WPOWER INFORMATION:|n

|y+powerinfo <discipline>/<power name>|n
|y+pinfo <discipline>/<power name>|n
  Get detailed information about a specific power.

|yExamples:|n
  +powerinfo animalism/bond famulus
  +pinfo celerity/cat's grace

Shows:
  - Power level requirement
  - Full description
  - Dice pool (if applicable)
  - Duration and effects
  - Whether you can currently use it

|WPOWER COSTS:|n

Most powers require a |WRouse check|n when activated:
  - Roll 1 die
  - On 1-5: Hunger increases by 1
  - On 6-10: No Hunger increase

Some powers are free to use (passive or low-level).

|WRESONANCE BONUSES:|n

If you have the right resonance from feeding, you gain
|g+1 die|n to powers that match:

  |ycholeric|n - Celerity, Potence powers
  |ymelancholic|n - Fortitude, Obfuscate powers
  |yphlegmatic|n - Auspex, Dominate powers
  |ysanguine|n - Blood Sorcery, Presence powers

|WACTIVE EFFECTS:|n

When powers create ongoing effects, use |y+effects|n to view them.
Effects may last for:
  - |WScene|n - Until the scene ends
  - |WTurns|n - A specific number of combat turns
  - |WPermanent|n - Until manually ended

|WHUNGER RESTRICTIONS:|n

At |[RHunger 5|n, you cannot activate most discipline powers.
Feed before attempting to use your vampiric abilities.
