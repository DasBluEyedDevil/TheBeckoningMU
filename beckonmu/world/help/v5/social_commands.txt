|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                   SOCIAL & COTERIE COMMANDS                      |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Commands for managing coteries (vampire groups) and viewing
social standing in Kindred society.

|WCOTERIE OVERVIEW:|n

Coteries are groups of vampires who band together for mutual
benefit. They share resources and work toward common goals.

|WVIEWING COTERIE:|n

|y+coterie|n
  View your current coterie information.

|y+coterie/resources|n
  View coterie resources (domain, haven, herd, contacts).

|WCREATING A COTERIE:|n

|y+coterie/create <name>=<description>|n
  Create a new coterie (you become the leader).

|yExample:|n
  +coterie/create Night Watch=Protectors of the Elysium

|WMEMBERSHIP MANAGEMENT:|n

|y+coterie/invite <character>|n
  Invite someone to join your coterie.

|y+coterie/join <leader>|n
  Accept an invitation to join a coterie.

|y+coterie/leave|n
  Leave your current coterie.

|yExamples:|n
  +coterie/invite Bob
  +coterie/join Alice

|WLEADERSHIP COMMANDS:|n (Coterie leader only)

|y+coterie/promote <character>|n
  Promote a member to lieutenant rank.

|y+coterie/demote <character>|n
  Demote a lieutenant to regular member.

|y+coterie/kick <character>|n
  Remove a member from the coterie.

|y+coterie/disband|n
  Disband the entire coterie (cannot be undone).

|yExamples:|n
  +coterie/promote Alice
  +coterie/kick Bob

|WCOTERIE RESOURCES:|n (Leader only)

|y+coterie/resource <type>=<value>|n
  Set a coterie resource value (0-5).

|yResource Types:|n
  - |ydomain|n - Territory controlled by the coterie
  - |yhaven|n - Shared haven quality
  - |yherd|n - Shared feeding sources
  - |ycontacts|n - Shared information network

|yExample:|n
  +coterie/resource domain=3
  +coterie/resource haven=2

|WCOTERIE RANKS:|n

|yLeader|n
  - Created the coterie or was promoted
  - Can invite/kick members
  - Can promote/demote members
  - Can set resources
  - Can disband coterie

|yLieutenant|n
  - Trusted members
  - May have special privileges (per ST)

|yMember|n
  - Standard membership
  - Benefits from shared resources

|WSOCIAL STANDING:|n

|y+social|n
|y+social <character>|n
  View comprehensive social standing.

Shows in one view:
  - Status (political standing in sect)
  - Boons (favors owed and held)
  - Coterie membership

|yExamples:|n
  +social              # View your own social standing
  +social Alice        # View Alice's public social info

|WSOCIAL MECHANICS:|n

Social standing in V5 is determined by:

|yStatus|n
  Political power within your sect (Camarilla, Anarch, etc.)
  Gained through positions, actions, and recognition.

|yBoons|n
  Favors owed to or held over other Kindred.
  See |yhelp boons|n for details on the boon system.

|yCoterie|n
  Strength in numbers - coterie resources can supplement
  individual weaknesses.
