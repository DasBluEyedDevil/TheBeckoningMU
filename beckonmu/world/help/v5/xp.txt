|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                     EXPERIENCE POINT SYSTEM                       |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Experience Points (XP) allow you to improve your character over time, buying
new dots in Attributes, Skills, Disciplines, and other traits.

|WVIEWING YOUR XP:|n

|y+xp|n
  - View your current XP total and XP log
  - See recent XP gains and expenditures

|y+xp/costs|n
  - Display the XP costs for all trait types
  - Quick reference for character advancement planning

|WSPENDING XP:|n

|y+spend <trait>=<dots>|n
  - Spend XP to increase a trait
  - Example: +spend Strength=3 (buy Strength to 3 dots)
  - Example: +spend Auspex=2 (buy Auspex to 2 dots)

|yTrait Types:|n
  - Attributes (Strength, Dexterity, Stamina, etc.)
  - Skills (Athletics, Academics, Streetwise, etc.)
  - Disciplines (Animalism, Auspex, Celerity, etc.)
  - Backgrounds (Resources, Allies, Contacts, etc.)

|WXP COSTS:|n

|yAttributes:|n
  - New Dot x 5 XP
  - Example: Strength 2 to 3 = 15 XP

|ySkills:|n
  - New Dot x 3 XP
  - Example: Academics 1 to 2 = 6 XP

|yIn-Clan Disciplines:|n
  - New Dot x 5 XP
  - Example: Auspex 2 to 3 = 15 XP (for Malkavian)

|yOut-of-Clan Disciplines:|n
  - New Dot x 7 XP
  - Example: Potence 1 to 2 = 14 XP (for Malkavian)

|yBackgrounds:|n
  - New Dot x 3 XP
  - Example: Resources 2 to 3 = 9 XP

|yClan/Generation:|n
  - Cannot be purchased with XP (story only)

|WEARNING XP:|n

XP is awarded by staff for:
  - |wActive Roleplay|n - Regular attendance and quality RP (weekly)
  - |wStory Participation|n - Participating in plots and events
  - |wCharacter Development|n - Deep character moments and growth
  - |wCommunity Contribution|n - Helping new players, running scenes
  - |wCreativity|n - Exceptional logs, wiki contributions, artwork

|yTypical Awards:|n
  - 2-3 XP per week for active play
  - 1-5 XP bonus for exceptional roleplay
  - 5-10 XP for major story conclusions

|WADVANCEMENT GUIDELINES:|n

- You can only raise a trait by 1 dot at a time
- Some traits require IC justification (new Disciplines, etc.)
- Staff may request roleplay before approving certain purchases
- Check |y+xp/costs|n before spending to avoid mistakes

|WEXAMPLES:|n

|yBuy Strength from 2 to 3:|n
  +spend Strength=3
  Cost: 15 XP (New Dot 3 x 5)

|yBuy Academics from 1 to 2:|n
  +spend Academics=2
  Cost: 6 XP (New Dot 2 x 3)

|yBuy in-clan Auspex from 2 to 3:|n
  +spend Auspex=3
  Cost: 15 XP (New Dot 3 x 5, Malkavian in-clan)

|yBuy out-of-clan Fortitude from 0 to 1:|n
  +spend Fortitude=1
  Cost: 7 XP (New Dot 1 x 7, not Malkavian in-clan)

|yView all costs:|n
  +xp/costs

|WSTAFF COMMANDS:|n (Staff only)

|y+xpaward <player>=<amount>/<reason>|n
  - Award XP to a player
  - Example: +xpaward Marcus=3/Excellent roleplay this week
