|cSTEP 1: CREATE YOUR CHARACTER|n

You have two options for character creation:

|wWeb-based Chargen|n (Recommended for new players)
  Visit: |yhttp://localhost:4001/character-creation/|n
  - Visual interface with tooltips
  - Validation to prevent errors
  - Submit for staff approval when complete

|wIn-Game Chargen|n
  Command: |w+chargen/start|n
  - Text-based step-by-step process
  - Type |w+chargen/help|n for guidance
  - Finalize with |w+chargen/finalize|n

|cSTEP 2: LEARN THE BASICS|n

|yEssential Commands|n

  |w+help|n              - Access help system
  |w+sheet|n             - View your character sheet
  |w+who|n               - See who's online
  |w+where|n             - See where players are (if implemented)
  |wlook|n               - Look at your surroundings
  |winventory|n          - Check your inventory

|yCommunication|n

  |wpage <name>=<msg>|n  - Send private message to player
  |w+bbs|n               - Read bulletin boards
  |w+bbs/post <board>/<subject>=<message>|n - Post to boards
  |wsay <text>|n         - Speak in current room
  |wpose <action>|n      - Describe your actions

|cSTEP 3: UNDERSTAND V5 MECHANICS|n

|yCore Systems|n

  |w+roll <pool>|n       - Roll dice (e.g., +roll strength + brawl)
  |w+rouse|n             - Make a Rouse check to awaken blood
  |w+hunt|n              - Feed on mortals
  |w+hunger|n            - Check your Hunger level
  |w+humanity|n          - View Humanity, Stains, Convictions

|yDisciplines (Vampire Powers)|n

  |w+disciplines|n       - List your disciplines
  |w+power <name>|n      - Activate a discipline power
  |w+powerinfo <name>|n  - Get info about a discipline power

|yCombat (when needed)|n

  |w+attack <target>|n   - Attack someone
  |w+dodge|n             - Dodge an attack
  |w+soak|n              - Soak damage
  |w+heal|n              - Spend blood to heal

|cKEY CONCEPTS FOR NEW PLAYERS|n

|yThe Hunger|n
- Ranges from 0 (satiated) to 5 (ravenous)
- Increases when you use Disciplines or take damage
- Feed with |w+hunt|n to reduce Hunger
- At Hunger 5, risk of frenzy is extreme

|yThe Masquerade|n
- NEVER reveal your vampire nature to mortals
- Violations = Blood Hunt (other vampires hunt you)
- Use Disciplines discreetly
- Clean up after feeding

|yHumanity & Stains|n
- Humanity measures your moral compass (1-10)
- Stains accumulate from immoral acts
- Roll Remorse at session end with |w+remorse|n
- Lose Humanity if you fail Remorse roll

|yFrenzy|n
- The Beast taking control
- Triggered by fire, hunger, provocation
- Resist with |w+frenzy/resist <difficulty>|n
- Consequences can be severe
