|cTHE WORLD OF DARKNESS|n

TheBeckoningMU is set in the World of Darkness, a reflection of our
modern world but darker, more Gothic, and tinged with supernatural
horror. Vampires exist in the shadows, hiding from mortal society
while waging their eternal Jyhad.

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|cVAMPIRE: THE MASQUERADE V5|n

This game uses the |yVampire: The Masquerade 5th Edition|n rules, which
emphasize:

  • |rThe Beast|n - Your inner predator, always hungry
  • |rHunger|n - The gnawing need for blood that defines unlife
  • |rThe Masquerade|n - Hiding vampire existence from mortals
  • |rPersonal Horror|n - The cost of immortality on your Humanity
  • |rPolitical Intrigue|n - Camarilla power struggles and Status
  • |rModern Nights|n - Contemporary setting with smartphones and CCTV

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|cKEY THEMES|n

|yThe Second Inquisition|n
The SI is a modern-day vampire hunting organization with government
resources. They've destroyed vampire havens, exposed Kindred, and
forced the Camarilla into hiding.

|yThe Beckoning|n
Ancient vampires feel an irresistible call to the Middle East. Many
Methuselahs and elders have abandoned their domains, leaving a power
vacuum.

|yBlood and Resonance|n
Feeding is personal and dangerous. The blood you drink shapes your
powers. Killing your prey leaves Stains on your Humanity.

|yThe Camarilla Endures|n
Despite losses, the Camarilla adapts. Princes still rule cities.
Status still matters. The Traditions are still enforced - especially
the Masquerade.

|cCLANS AVAILABLE|n

All 13 V5 core clans are available for play:

  |wCamarilla|n: Ventrue, Toreador, Tremere, Malkavian, Nosferatu, Brujah
  |wIndependent|n: Banu Haqim, Gangrel, Ravnos, Hecata, Lasombra
  |wAnarch|n: Ministry (Followers of Set), Tzimisce
  |wClanless|n: Caitiff, Thin-Bloods

Each clan has unique Disciplines, Banes, and Compulsions that shape
gameplay and roleplay.
