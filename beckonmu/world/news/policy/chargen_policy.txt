|c1. CHARACTER APPROVAL PROCESS|n

|yApproval Required|n
- ALL characters must be approved by staff before play
- Use web chargen OR in-game |w+chargen|n system
- Submit with |w+chargen/finalize|n or web "Submit for Approval"
- Approval typically within 24-48 hours

|yWhat Staff Reviews|n
- Concept fits V5 Vampire theme
- Mechanical stats are legal and balanced
- Background is realistic and grounded
- Character doesn't break game continuity
- No "special snowflake" concepts without approval

|yRevisions|n
- Staff may request changes before approval
- Check your |w+jobs|n for staff feedback
- Resubmit after making requested changes
- Usually approved within 1-2 revision rounds

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c2. CHARACTER CONCEPT GUIDELINES|n

|yAppropriate Concepts|n
- Newly-Embraced vampires (neonates)
- Thin-Bloods (Duskborn)
- Ancillae (100-300 years old, with strong justification)
- Characters who fit the V5 modern nights setting
- Concepts that create RP opportunities for others

|yForbidden Concepts|n
- Methuselahs or Elders (1000+ years, too powerful)
- Canon characters from published books (Beckett, Hardestadt, etc.)
- Children of canon characters (without exceptional justification)
- Diablerists (without plot approval)
- Sabbat vampires (NPC faction in V5)
- Werewolves, Mages, or other supernatural types (Vampire game only)

|yGrey Area (Requires Staff Approval)|n
- Ancillae (older than 100 years)
- Characters with exotic bloodlines
- Characters involved in major historical events
- Characters with unusual merits or flaws
- Cross-clan disciplines
- Characters related to other PCs

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c3. MECHANICAL GUIDELINES|n

|yStarting Character Stats|n

|wAttributes:|n
- 1 dot in all attributes for free
- Assign 7/5/3 dots across Physical/Social/Mental
- No attribute above 3 at character creation (rare exceptions)

|wSkills:|n
- Assign 13/9/5 dots across Talents/Skills/Knowledges
- No skill above 3 at character creation (without justification)
- At least one specialty (dot represents specialized training)

|wDisciplines:|n
- 2 dots in-clan disciplines (e.g., 2/0/0 or 1/1/0)
- Caitiff: 2 dots in any disciplines (with justification)
- Thin-Bloods: Thin-Blood Alchemy instead
- Out-of-clan disciplines require strong IC justification

|wBackgrounds:|n
- 3 dots total to distribute
- Resources 3+ requires justification (you're rich!)
- Herd 3+ means significant mortal blood supply
- Allies/Contacts: Define who they are

|wAdvantages:|n
- Humanity starts at 7 (adjust with merits/flaws if desired)
- Willpower = Composure + Resolve (max 10)
- Health levels standard for vampires
- Blood Potency 0-1 for neonates

|wMerits & Flaws:|n
- Balanced - don't min-max
- Must have RP justification
- Some require staff approval (check merit descriptions)

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c4. BACKGROUND WRITING|n

|yRequired Elements|n
- Mortal life: Who were you before the Embrace?
- The Embrace: How and why were you turned?
- Adjustment: How did you adapt to being a vampire?
- Recent history: What have you been doing lately?
- Motivation: Why are you in the city? What do you want?

|yLength Guidelines|n
- Minimum: 500 words
- Recommended: 1000-1500 words
- Maximum: No limit, but be concise

|yGood Background Practices|n
- Grounded and realistic
- Leaves hooks for staff plots
- Creates connections to other PCs (with their permission)
- Explains why you're in the city now
- Shows character growth and motivation

|yBackground Red Flags (Avoid These)|n
- "Most powerful X in the world"
- Killed an Elder single-handedly
- Secret royal bloodline
- Trained by famous canon NPCs
- Extensive tragic backstory with no flaws
- No connections to anything/anyone (lone wolf)

|c5. CLAN SELECTION|n

|yAvailable Clans|n
All 13 V5 core clans are available:

|wCamarilla:|n Ventrue, Toreador, Tremere, Malkavian, Nosferatu, Brujah
|wIndependent:|n Banu Haqim, Gangrel, Ravnos, Hecata, Lasombra
|wAnarch:|n Ministry, Tzimisce
|wClanless:|n Caitiff, Thin-Bloods

|yConsider Before Choosing|n
- Clan banes and how they affect RP
- In-clan disciplines and what powers interest you
- Clan culture and whether it fits your concept
- Clan stereotypes vs. your character's personality

|yDifficult Clans for New Players|n
- |yMalkavian|n: Requires maturity to play mental illness well
- |yNosferatu|n: Social penalty (Hideous) limits some RP
- |yTzimisce|n: Complex mechanics and alien mindset
- |yHecata|n: Death-focused, niche roleplay

|yGood Starter Clans|n
- |gVentrue|n: Leadership, political play
- |gToreador|n: Social, artistic, passionate
- |gBrujah|n: Rebellious, physical, passionate
- |gGangrel|n: Survivalist, independent, adaptable

|c6. PREDATOR TYPE|n

|yRequired Choice|n
- ALL characters must choose a Predator Type
- Defines how you feed and your relationship with Hunger
- Affects starting dots in certain skills/disciplines

|yAvailable Predator Types|n
- |wAlleycat|n - Street predator, opportunistic feeding
- |wBagger|n - Feed on blood bags, hospitals
- |wBlood Leech|n - Feed on other vampires
- |wClercestory|n - Feed on your herd, sustainable
- |wConsensusalist|n - Feed only with permission
- |wFarmer|n - Feed on animals
- |wOsmosis|n - Feed during sex
- |wSandman|n - Break into homes, sleeping victims
- |wSiren|n - Seduction and willing victims
- |wScene Queen|n - Feed at clubs and parties

|yConsiderations|n
- How does this fit your Humanity?
- What are the risks of your feeding style?
- How does it affect your nightly routine?

|c7. NAME & DESCRIPTION|n

|yName Guidelines|n
- Appropriate for character's cultural background
- Not obviously silly or joke names
- Not names of famous people (real or fictional)
- Nicknames/aliases are fine for everyday use

|yDescription Guidelines|n
- Physical appearance: height, build, features
- Fashion sense and typical attire
- Mannerisms and body language
- First impression others get
- Minimum 200 words, no maximum

|yVampire-Specific Details|n
- Apparent age (age at Embrace)
- Any visible signs of clan (Nosferatu especially)
- How you hide fangs in public
- Mortal disguise vs. true appearance

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c8. CONVICTIONS & TOUCHSTONES|n

|yConvictions (Max 3)|n
- Personal moral code
- What lines won't you cross?
- What do you value most?
- Examples: "Never harm children", "Protect the weak", "Always repay debts"

|yTouchstones (Max = Humanity ÷ 2)|n
- Mortals who anchor your Humanity
- People you care about
- Connections to mortal world
- Must define name and relationship
- Examples: Sister, childhood friend, former lover, priest

|yImportance|n
- These drive Humanity mechanics
- Losing Touchstones = Humanity loss potential
- Convictions guide when you gain Stains

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c9. STARTING EQUIPMENT|n

|yStandard Assumption|n
- Clothing appropriate to concept
- Basic personal items (phone, wallet, keys)
- Haven (based on Resources background)

|yResources Background|n
- Resources 0-1: Poor, struggling
- Resources 2-3: Middle class, comfortable
- Resources 4-5: Rich, wealthy (requires justification)

|yWeapons|n
- Basic weapons okay (knife, small firearm)
- Serious weapons require justification
- Illegal weapons need good backstory reason

|yNo Starting Equipment|n
- Magical items
- Elder artifacts
- Experimental SI tech
- Anything game-breaking

|cFINAL NOTES|n

|yBe Patient|n
- Character creation takes time - don't rush!
- Quality concept > min-maxed stats
- Your character will grow through play

|yBe Creative|n
- Make a character YOU want to play
- Don't copy concepts from books or other media
- Find your unique angle

|yBe Collaborative|n
- Leave hooks for staff and other players
- Be open to connections with other PCs
- Think about how your character fits the larger story
