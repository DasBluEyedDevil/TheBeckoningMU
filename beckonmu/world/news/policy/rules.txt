|c1. BE RESPECTFUL|n

|yOut of Character (OOC)|n
- Treat all players with respect and courtesy
- No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Disagreements should be handled civilly
- What happens IC stays IC - don't take IC conflicts OOC

|yIn Character (IC)|n
- Vampire society is brutal, but players should consent to conflict
- Communicate OOC before major character-affecting scenes
- Follow the "Yes, and..." improv principle when possible

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c2. THE MASQUERADE IS PARAMOUNT|n

|yCore Principle|n
- Vampires MUST hide their existence from mortals
- Violations of the Masquerade have severe IC consequences
- The Second Inquisition is always watching

|yBreaking the Masquerade|n
- Using obvious supernatural powers in public
- Feeding without cleaning up evidence
- Leaving drained corpses to be discovered
- Being recorded on camera during supernatural acts

|yIC Consequences|n
- Blood Hunt called by the Prince
- Other vampires hunt and destroy you
- No appeals, no mercy

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c3. CONSENT & MATURE THEMES|n

|yMature Content|n
- This game contains horror, violence, and mature themes
- Players must be 18+ (enforced)
- Content warnings should be given for extreme scenes

|yConsent-Based Conflict|n
- |wCharacter Death|n requires OOC consent
- |wMajor Character Changes|n require OOC consent
- |wRomance/ERP|n requires mutual OOC consent
- Players can say "fade to black" at any time

|yForbidden Content|n
- Sexual content involving minors (zero tolerance)
- Graphic sexual violence (discuss with staff first)
- Real-world hate group imagery or propaganda
- OOC harassment disguised as IC action

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c4. CHARACTER CREATION & APPROVAL|n

|yCharacter Approval Process|n
- Submit character via web interface or |w+chargen/finalize|n
- Staff reviews for theme compliance and balance
- Approval typically within 24-48 hours
- Revisions may be requested

|yCharacter Concept Guidelines|n
- Characters must fit V5 Vampire theme
- No "special snowflake" concepts without staff approval
- Backgrounds must be realistic and grounded
- Power level appropriate to starting characters

|yForbidden Concepts|n
- Methuselahs or Elders (too powerful)
- Characters from published V5 books (unless approved)
- Canon character relatives (without strong justification)
- Concepts that break game balance

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c5. ROLEPLAY EXPECTATIONS|n

|yQuality Over Quantity|n
- Focus on meaningful character development
- Quality poses over rapid-fire one-liners
- Give other players material to respond to

|yActivity Requirements|n
- Log in at least once per week (minimum)
- Notify staff of extended absences via |w+job|n
- Inactive characters (30+ days) may be frozen

|yScene Etiquette|n
- Don't interrupt ongoing scenes without invitation
- Respect posting order in multi-player scenes
- If you must leave early, give OOC notice

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c6. POWER USAGE & MECHANICS|n

|yDiscipline Usage|n
- All discipline use requires proper commands (|w+power|n)
- Describe HOW you use powers in your poses
- Respect the rules for Rouse checks and Hunger

|yDice Rolling|n
- Use |w+roll|n for all contested actions
- Don't cherry-pick good rolls or re-roll without ST approval
- Dice results are binding unless ST rules otherwise

|yCombat|n
- Use the combat system (|w+attack|n, |w+dodge|n, etc.)
- No god-moding (controlling others' actions)
- Accept dice results, even if unfavorable

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c7. EXPERIENCE & ADVANCEMENT|n

|yEarning XP|n
- XP awarded weekly for activity
- Bonus XP for excellent RP, helping new players, running plots
- XP rates posted on |w+bbs policy|n

|ySpending XP|n
- Use |w+xp/spend|n to improve traits
- IC justification required for all purchases
- Some purchases require ST approval (new Disciplines, etc.)

|yPower Limits|n
- Starting characters have appropriate power levels
- Elder-level powers require years of play and ST approval
- No "power grinding" - RP must justify growth

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c8. STAFF INTERACTION|n

|yStaff Authority|n
- Staff decisions are final
- Appeals can be made respectfully via |w+job|n
- Don't argue with staff in public channels

|yStaff Requests|n
- Use |w+job/create|n for all staff requests
- Be specific and provide relevant information
- Be patient - staff are volunteers

|yReporting Issues|n
- Report rule violations via |w+job|n immediately
- Don't take enforcement into your own hands
- Staff will investigate and take appropriate action

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c9. MULTI-CHAR & ALT POLICY|n

|yMultiple Characters|n
- Players may have up to 2 active characters
- Characters must be in different spheres/coteries
- No IC interaction between your own characters

|yAlt Etiquette|n
- Don't use OOC information from one char on another
- Don't use alts to spy or gain advantages
- Violations result in all characters being removed

|w═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════|n

|c10. CONSEQUENCES & ENFORCEMENT|n

|yViolations|n
- Minor: Warning and education
- Moderate: Temporary suspension (1-7 days)
- Major: Character removal or site ban
- Severe: Permanent ban

|yZero Tolerance|n
- Sexual content involving minors
- OOC harassment or threats
- Cheating or exploiting code bugs
- Real-world discrimination or hate speech
