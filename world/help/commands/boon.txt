|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                        BOON COMMANDS                              |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Manage the intricate web of favors and debts in Camarilla society.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+boon|n                    - View your boons (owed and owing)
  |y+boon <character>|n        - View someone's public boon ledger
  |y+boongive <char>=<type>/<reason>|n - Offer a boon
  |y+boonaccept <id>|n         - Accept a pending boon offer
  |y+boondecline <id>|n        - Decline a pending boon offer
  |y+booncall <id>|n           - Call in a boon you're owed
  |y+boonfulfill <id>|n        - Fulfill a called boon
  |y+boonadmin|n               - Admin boon management (staff only)

|WBOON TYPES:|n

  |yTrivial|n - Minor favors, casual assistance
  |yMinor|n - Standard favors, significant help
  |yMajor|n - Substantial favors, serious obligations
  |yBlood|n - Life-saving acts, grievous debts
  |yLife|n - Ultimate debt, almost never repaid

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+boon|n
    View all your boons (what you owe and what you're owed)

  |y+boon Alice|n
    View Alice's public boon ledger

  |y+boongive Bob=minor/provided alibi for Elysium incident|n
    Offer Bob a Minor boon

  |y+boonaccept 5|n
    Accept pending boon offer #5

  |y+boondecline 7|n
    Decline pending boon offer #7

  |y+booncall 3|n
    Call in boon #3 that someone owes you

  |y+boonfulfill 8|n
    Mark boon #8 as fulfilled

|WHOW BOONS WORK:|n

1. |yOffer:|n Character A offers Character B a boon
2. |yAccept:|n Character B accepts (now owes the boon)
3. |yCall:|n Character A calls in the boon when needed
4. |yFulfill:|n Character B performs the favor and fulfills it

|WBOON LEDGER:|n

Your ledger tracks:
  - |yBoons Owed:|n What you owe to others (your debts)
  - |yBoons Owing:|n What others owe you (your credits)
  - |yPending:|n Offers awaiting acceptance/decline
  - |yCalled:|n Boons that have been invoked but not fulfilled

|WBOON POLITICS:|n

  - Boons are |ythe primary currency|n of Camarilla politics
  - |[RRefusing|n to honor a boon is a grievous insult
  - The |yHarpy|n officiates disputes and tracks major boons
  - Boons can be |ytraded|n between vampires
  - Some use boons to control others politically

|WBOON WEIGHT:|n

Each boon type has a different weight:
  - |yTrivial:|n 1 point
  - |yMinor:|n 2 points
  - |yMajor:|n 5 points
  - |yBlood:|n 10 points
  - |yLife:|n 20 points

Multiple smaller boons can equal one larger boon.

|WSOCIAL IMPLICATIONS:|n

  - Owing boons can damage your reputation
  - Being owed boons increases your influence
  - Strategic boon management is key to power
  - Never forget who owes you - and who you owe

|WNOTES:|n

  - Always document significant favors as boons
  - The Harpy witnesses important boon transactions
  - Boon disputes can be brought before the Prince
  - Staff can adjudicate complex boon situations
