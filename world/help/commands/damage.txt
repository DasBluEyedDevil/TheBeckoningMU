|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                        DAMAGE COMMAND                             |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Apply damage to a target after a successful attack.

|WUSAGE:|n

  |y+damage <target>=<amount>|n            - Apply superficial damage
  |y+damage <target>=<amount>/<type>|n    - Apply specific damage type

|WDAMAGE TYPES:|n

  |ySuperficial|n - Minor damage (default for vampires)
    - Heals quickly via Blood Surge
    - Fills health boxes from left to right
    - Examples: Brawling, bashing weapons

  |yLethal|n - Serious damage
    - Converts to superficial for vampires (undead resilience)
    - Mortals take lethal as-is
    - Examples: Gunshots, stab wounds

  |yAggravated|n - Severe, supernatural damage
    - Very difficult to heal
    - Fills health boxes from right to left
    - Examples: Fire, sunlight, claws, vampire fangs

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+damage Bob=3|n
    Apply 3 superficial damage to Bob

  |y+damage Alice=2/aggravated|n
    Apply 2 aggravated damage to Alice

  |y+damage Charlie=5/lethal|n
    Apply 5 lethal damage to Charlie

|WDAMAGE CALCULATION:|n

Typical damage formula:
  Base weapon damage |y+|n Margin of success |y+|n Discipline bonuses

|yUnarmed (Brawl):|n 0 + margin + Potence
|yKnife:|n 1 + margin + Potence
|ySword:|n 2 + margin + Potence
|yPistol:|n 2 + margin
|yRifle:|n 3 + margin

|WHEALTH TRACKER:|n

Characters have Health = |yStamina + 3|n boxes:
  - |wO|n = Healthy (undamaged)
  - |y/|n = Superficial damage
  - |[RX|n = Aggravated damage

Example: |w[O O |y/ / |[RX X|w]|n
  - 2 healthy, 2 superficial, 2 aggravated

|WIMPAIRMENT:|n

|WSPECIAL CASES:|n

|yOverfill Superficial:|n
  - If all boxes filled with superficial, additional superficial converts to aggravated

|yFortitude Soak:|n
  - Active Fortitude powers may reduce incoming damage

|yArmor:|n
  - Some armor provides damage reduction (ST adjudication)

|WTORPOR (VAMPIRES):|n

When all Health boxes filled:
  - |gAll Superficial:|n Enter Torpor (death-like sleep)
  - |[RAny Aggravated:|n Final Death (character destroyed)

|WDEATH (MORTALS):|n

Mortals die when all Health boxes filled with Lethal or Aggravated damage.

|WNOTES:|n

  - Use |y+heal|n to remove damage
  - Storytellers apply damage based on narrative and rolls
  - Combat damage should have lasting consequences
  - Consider surrender before reaching Torpor/Death
