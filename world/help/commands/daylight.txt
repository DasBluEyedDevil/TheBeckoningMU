|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                  THIN-BLOOD DAYLIGHT POWER                        |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Thin-Blood merit allowing limited daytime activity.

|WUSAGE:|n

  |y+daylight|n              - Check daylight resistance status
  |y+daylight/activate|n     - Activate daylight protection (if available)
  |y+daylight/deactivate|n   - Deactivate protection

|WOVERVIEW:|n

Thin-Bloods with the |yDaywalker|n merit can resist the sun's lethal
effects to a limited degree. This power represents their weakened
vampire nature - a blessing and a curse.

|WREQUIREMENTS:|n

  - Must be |yThin-Blood|n clan
  - Must have |yDaywalker|n merit (purchased during chargen or with XP)
  - Merit levels determine protection strength

|WDAYWALKER MERIT LEVELS:|n

  |y1 Dot|n - Twilight Walker
    - Tolerate dawn/dusk (indirect sunlight)
    - Can be active 1 hour before/after sunrise/sunset
    - Direct sunlight still causes aggravated damage

  |y2 Dots|n - Overcast Days
    - Tolerate cloudy/overcast daytime
    - Can operate during heavy cloud cover
    - Direct sunlight in clear weather still dangerous

  |y3 Dots|n - Full Daywalker
    - Nearly immune to sunlight
    - Can operate in full daylight
    - Extended exposure (hours) may still cause fatigue

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+daylight|n
    Check your current daylight protection status

  |y+daylight/activate|n
    Activate your Daywalker protection

  |y+daylight/deactivate|n
    Deactivate protection (conserve merit use if limited)

|WSUNLIGHT EFFECTS:|n

|yFor Normal Vampires:|n
  - |[RDirect sunlight:|n 2-5 Aggravated damage per turn
  - |[RExtended exposure:|n Final Death (complete immolation)
  - |[RInstinctive terror:|n Rötschreck (terror frenzy)

|yFor Thin-Bloods (no merit):|n
  - Same as normal vampires
  - Sunlight is just as lethal
  - Must shelter during the day

|yFor Daywalkers:|n
  - Protection based on merit level
  - May still take reduced damage
  - No Rötschreck terror frenzy
  - Can engage in daytime activities

|WLIMITATIONS:|n

  - Daywalker is |yThin-Blood exclusive|n
  - Other vampires |ycannot|n learn this
  - Higher-generation vampires sometimes resent this ability
  - May draw unwanted attention from vampire hunters
  - Still suffer other vampire weaknesses (blood, hunger, etc.)

|WTACTICAL ADVANTAGES:|n

  |yDaytime Operations:|n
    - Conduct business during mortal hours
    - Investigate while enemies sleep
    - Escape pursuit by going out in daylight

  |ySocial Benefits:|n
    - Easier to maintain mortal connections
    - Can work mortal jobs
    - Less suspicious to mortals

  |yStrategic Value:|n
    - Gather intelligence while others shelter
    - Daytime meetings with mortal contacts
    - Emergency escape route

|WROLEPLAY CONSIDERATIONS:|n

  - Other vampires may be jealous or suspicious
  - Elders see this as proof of your thin blood
  - Camarilla may try to exploit your ability
  - You're still weak in other ways (Blood Potency 0)
  - Balance this advantage against thin-blood disadvantages

|WNOTES:|n

  - This power is part of the Thin-Blood Merit system
  - Cannot be learned by other clan vampires
  - Storytellers adjudicate edge cases (solar eclipses, etc.)
  - May have plot implications - use wisely
  - Staff approval required for exceptional circumstances
