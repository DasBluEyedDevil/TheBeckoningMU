|W║                       FEEDING COMMAND                             |
|[R╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗|n

Feed on mortal prey to reduce your Hunger.

|WUSAGE:|n

  |yfeed <target> [<resonance>]|n
  |yfeed/slake <target> [<resonance>]|n

|WARGUMENTS:|n

  <target>     - The mortal you're feeding on
  <resonance>  - Optional: choleric, melancholic, phlegmatic, sanguine

|WSWITCHES:|n

  |y/slake|n - Feed until Hunger reaches 0 (multiple rolls, very risky!)

|WRESONANCE TYPES:|n

  |yCholeric|n     - Anger, aggression (affects violent traits)
  |yMelancholic|n  - Sadness, depression (affects introspective traits)
  |yPhlegmatic|n   - Calm, apathy (affects composed traits)
  |ySanguine|n     - Joy, passion (affects social traits)

Resonance choice affects your character's emotional state and can
provide bonuses to related rolls.

|WFEEDING MECHANICS:|n

1. Roll |yStrength + Brawl|n (modified by Predator Type)
2. Difficulty is usually 2
3. Your current Hunger level determines Hunger dice risk

|gSuccess (1-2 successes):|n
  - Reduce Hunger by 1 point
  - Set resonance if specified
  - Safe, controlled feeding

|gSuccess (3+ successes):|n
  - Reduce Hunger by 2-3 points
  - Set resonance if specified
  - Very successful hunt

|[RFailure:|n
  - No Hunger reduction
  - Possible complications
  - Victim may escape or alert others

|[RMessy Critical (all 10s on Hunger dice):|n
  - Feeding frenzy
  - Kill the victim
  - Masquerade risk
  - Gain Stains

|[RBestial Failure (all 1s on Hunger dice):|n
  - Trigger Compulsion
  - Possible frenzy
  - Hunting attempt fails

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |yfeed mortal|n
    Feed on a mortal with no specific resonance

  |yfeed mortal choleric|n
    Feed on an angry mortal (choleric resonance)

  |yfeed/slake mortal sanguine|n
    Feed to Hunger 0 on a joyful mortal (DANGEROUS!)

|WSLAKE MODE:|n

Using |y/slake|n makes multiple feeding rolls until Hunger reaches 0.
This is |[REXTREMELY DANGEROUS|n:
  - Each roll risks Messy Critical
  - Multiple kills possible
  - High Masquerade breach risk
  - Multiple Stains likely

Only use in emergencies or when safely isolated!

|WNOTES:|n

  - Cannot feed at Hunger 0
  - Feeding while witnessed risks Masquerade breach
  - Killing mortals adds Stains (Humanity loss risk)
  - Your Predator Type affects feeding effectiveness
  - Resonance persists and affects future rolls
  - Use |yhunger|n command to check current Hunger level
