|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                         HEAL COMMAND                              |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Heal damage on yourself or a target.

|WUSAGE:|n

  |y+heal <target>=<amount>|n            - Heal superficial damage
  |y+heal <target>=<amount>/<type>|n    - Heal specific damage type
  |y+heal self=<amount>|n                - Heal yourself

|WDAMAGE TYPES:|n

  |ySuperficial|n - Heals quickly
    - Vampires: Blood Surge (Rouse check)
    - Mortals: First aid, rest

  |yAggravated|n - Heals slowly
    - Vampires: Extended torpor or rituals
    - Mortals: Extended medical care

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+heal self=2|n
    Heal 2 superficial damage on yourself

  |y+heal Bob=3/superficial|n
    Heal 3 superficial damage on Bob

  |y+heal Alice=1/aggravated|n
    Heal 1 aggravated damage on Alice (requires permission)

|WHEALING METHODS (VAMPIRES):|n

|yBlood Surge:|n
  - Rouse check to activate
  - Heals superficial damage based on Blood Potency:
    * BP 0-1: 2 superficial per surge
    * BP 2-3: 3 superficial per surge
    * BP 4-5: 4 superficial per surge
  - Can be used multiple times per scene

|yExtended Rest:|n
  - Heal 1 superficial per night of rest
  - Requires safe haven and no activity

|yTorpor (for Aggravated):|n
  - Enter voluntary torpor to heal aggravated damage
  - Duration depends on Humanity and Blood Potency
  - Requires safe location

|yBlood Sorcery:|n
  - Certain rituals can heal aggravated damage
  - Requires Blood Sorcery discipline
  - May have additional costs

|WHEALING METHODS (MORTALS):|n

|yFirst Aid:|n
  - Intelligence + Medicine roll
  - Heals 1-2 superficial damage

|yMedical Care:|n
  - Hospital or trained physician
  - Heals lethal and some aggravated over time

|yNatural Healing:|n
  - 1 superficial per day of rest
  - Weeks/months for aggravated

|WHEALING RESTRICTIONS:|n

  - You can always heal yourself (within rules)
  - Healing others may require:
    * Their consent
    * Appropriate powers/skills
    * Staff permission for major healing
  - Aggravated damage is |[Rdifficult|n to heal
  - Some aggravated (fire/sunlight) may require specialized treatment

|WDISCIPLINE HEALING:|n

  |yFortitude:|n Some powers provide regeneration
  |yBlood Sorcery:|n Rituals can heal grievous wounds
  |yVitae:|n Vampire blood can heal mortals (but risks Blood Bond)

|WNOTES:|n

  - Healing should be justified narratively
  - Blood Surge is most common vampire healing method
  - Aggravated damage tells a story - don't rush healing it
  - Staff may adjudicate special healing circumstances
