|W║                        HEALTH COMMAND                             |
|[R╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗|n

View current health status for yourself or another character.

|WUSAGE:|n

  |y+health|n              - View your own health status
  |y+health <character>|n  - View another character's health status

|WDISPLAYED INFORMATION:|n

  |yHealth Boxes:|n Visual representation of current health
  |yDamage Breakdown:|n Amount of superficial vs aggravated damage
  |yImpairment Status:|n Whether you're suffering penalties
  |yHealing Options:|n Available healing methods

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+health|n
    Display your current health status

  |y+health Bob|n
    View Bob's health status (if visible)

|WHEALTH TRACKER:|n

Health boxes = |yStamina + 3|n

Each box can contain:
  - |wO|n = Healthy (no damage)
  - |y/|n = Superficial damage
  - |[RX|n = Aggravated damage

|WEXAMPLE OUTPUT:|n

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
│ HEALTH STATUS: Alice
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Health: |w[O |y/ / |[RX X X|w]|n

Maximum Health: 6 (Stamina 3 + 3)
Superficial Damage: 2
Aggravated Damage: 3
Healthy Boxes: 1

Status: |[RIMPAIRED|n (-2 dice to all actions)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

|WIMPAIRMENT:|n

When at ≤50% Health: |[R-2 dice penalty|n to all actions

This represents difficulty functioning while grievously injured.

Threshold = Total Health ÷ 2 (rounded down)

|WHEALING:|n

|ySuperficial Damage:|n
  - Vampires: Blood Surge (Rouse check)
  - Mortals: First aid, rest
  - Heals quickly (hours to days)

|yAggravated Damage:|n
  - Vampires: Extended torpor or rituals
  - Mortals: Extended medical care
  - Heals slowly (weeks to months)

Use |y+heal|n command to heal damage.

|WTORPOR WARNING:|n

When all Health boxes are filled with damage:
  - |yAll Superficial:|n Enter Torpor (death-like sleep)
  - |[RAny Aggravated:|n Final Death (character destroyed)

|WCOMBAT IMPLICATIONS:|n

  - Impaired characters are easier to hit
  - May want to flee when heavily damaged
  - Consider surrender before reaching Torpor
  - Aggravated damage is serious - avoid fire and fangs

|WNOTES:|n

  - You can always see your own health
  - Seeing others' health may require observation
  - Health affects all dice pools when Impaired
  - Tracking health is crucial in combat
