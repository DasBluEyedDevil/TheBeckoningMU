|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                      HUNTING COMMANDS                             |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Hunt for mortal prey to slake your Hunger.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+hunt <location>|n     - Begin hunting in a location
  |y+feed <target>|n       - Feed on a specific target (consensual)
  |y+huntinfo|n            - View hunting location information
  |y+huntaction <action>|n - Take action during active hunt
  |y+huntcancel|n          - Cancel your active hunt

|WHUNTING LOCATIONS:|n

  |yClub|n - Nightclubs and parties (Difficulty 3)
    - Loud music, crowds, easy prey
    - Predator bonus: Scene Queen

  |yStreet|n - Dark alleys and back streets (Difficulty 4)
    - Dangerous, unpredictable
    - Predator bonus: Alleycat

  |yResidential|n - Homes and apartments (Difficulty 5)
    - Private, requires breaking and entering
    - Predator bonus: Sandman

  |yHospital|n - Medical facilities (Difficulty 5)
    - Security, but access to blood bags
    - Predator bonus: Bagger

  |ySecured|n - Gated communities, secure buildings (Difficulty 6)
    - High security, wealthy prey
    - Predator bonus: Siren

  |yRural|n - Outskirts, wilderness (Difficulty 4)
    - Animals and isolated prey
    - Predator bonus: Farmer

|WHUNT FLOW:|n

1. |y+hunt <location>|n - Start hunting
2. System rolls your dice pool (usually Wits + appropriate skill)
3. On success: Find prey
4. |y+huntaction approach/ambush/seduce|n - Choose your method
5. Feed or face consequences

|WPREDATOR TYPE BONUSES:|n

Your Predator Type grants bonuses to specific hunting methods:
  - |yAlleycat:|n +2 dice on Street hunts
  - |yBagger:|n +2 dice at Hospital
  - |yScene Queen:|n +2 dice at Club
  - |ySandman:|n +2 dice in Residential
  - |ySiren:|n +2 dice in Secured areas
  - |yFarmer:|n +2 dice in Rural areas

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+hunt club|n
    Hunt for prey at a nightclub

  |y+huntinfo|n
    View all hunting locations and difficulties

  |y+huntaction seduce|n
    Attempt to seduce your prey

  |y+huntcancel|n
    Cancel your active hunt

  |y+feed Alice|n
    Feed on Alice (requires consent)

|WFEEDING RESULTS:|n

  - |gSuccess:|n Reduce Hunger by 1-2 (depending on prey quality)
  - |yPartial:|n Reduce Hunger by 1, possible complications
  - |[RFailure:|n No Hunger reduction, possible Masquerade breach

|WNOTES:|n

  - Hunting at Hunger 5 is |[Rdesperate and dangerous|n
  - Failed hunts may attract attention or cause Masquerade breaches
  - Some locations are riskier but offer better prey
  - Use your Herd background for easy, safe feeding
