|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                      HUNTING COMMANDS                             |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Hunt for mortal prey to slake your Hunger.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+hunt <location>|n          - Quick automated hunt
  |y+hunt/quick <location>|n    - Quick automated hunt (same as above)
  |y+hunt/staffed <location>|n  - Request staff-run hunt scene
  |y+huntinfo|n                 - View hunting location information
  |yfeed <resonance>|n          - Feed on hunted prey
  |yfeed/slake <resonance>|n    - Feed to Hunger 0

|WHUNTING LOCATIONS:|n

  |yClub|n - Nightclubs and parties (Difficulty 3)
    - Loud music, crowds, easy prey
    - Predator bonus: Scene Queen

  |yStreet|n - Dark alleys and back streets (Difficulty 4)
    - Dangerous, unpredictable
    - Predator bonus: Alleycat

  |yResidential|n - Homes and apartments (Difficulty 5)
    - Private, requires breaking and entering
    - Predator bonus: Sandman

  |yHospital|n - Medical facilities (Difficulty 6)
    - Security, but access to blood bags
    - Predator bonus: Bagger

  |ySecured|n - Gated communities, secure buildings (Difficulty 7)
    - High security, wealthy prey
    - Predator bonus: Siren

  |yRural|n - Outskirts, wilderness (Difficulty 4)
    - Animals and isolated prey
    - Predator bonus: Farmer

|WHUNT TYPES:|n

|yQuick Hunt|n (default)
  - Automated dice roll using appropriate skill
  - Immediate results
  - Reduces Hunger on success
  - Best for quick feeding

|yStaffed Hunt|n (use /staffed switch)
  - Creates a Job request for staff
  - Staff will run interactive hunt scene
  - More immersive roleplay experience
  - Staff contacts you when available

|WPREDATOR TYPE BONUSES:|n

Your Predator Type grants bonuses to specific hunting methods:
  - |yAlleycat:|n +2 dice on Street hunts (uses Stealth)
  - |yBagger:|n +2 dice at Hospital (uses Streetwise)
  - |yScene Queen:|n +2 dice at Club (uses Streetwise)
  - |ySandman:|n +2 dice in Residential (uses Stealth)
  - |ySiren:|n +2 dice in Secured areas (uses Persuasion)
  - |yConsensualist:|n +2 dice (uses Persuasion)
  - |yFarmer:|n +2 dice in Rural areas (uses Animal Ken)

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+hunt club|n
    Quick automated hunt at a nightclub

  |y+hunt/staffed residential|n
    Request staff to run a hunt scene in residential area

  |y+huntinfo|n
    View all hunting locations and difficulties

  |yfeed choleric|n
    Feed on prey, selecting Choleric resonance

  |yfeed/slake sanguine|n
    Feed to Hunger 0 with Sanguine resonance

|WFEEDING RESULTS:|n

  - |gSuccess (3+ successes):|n Reduce Hunger by 2-3 points
  - |ySuccess (1-2 successes):|n Reduce Hunger by 1 point
  - |[RFailure:|n No Hunger reduction, possible complications
  - |[RMessy Critical:|n Feeding frenzy, Masquerade risk
  - |[RBestial Failure:|n Compulsion or frenzy triggered

|WNOTES:|n

  - Hunting at Hunger 5 is |[Rdesperate and dangerous|n
  - Failed hunts may attract attention or cause Masquerade breaches
  - Some locations are riskier but offer better prey
  - Use your Herd background for easy, safe feeding
  - Resonance choices affect your character's traits
