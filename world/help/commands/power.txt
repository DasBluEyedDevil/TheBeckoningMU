|W║                     DISCIPLINE POWERS                             |
|[R╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗|n

Activate and manage your vampire's supernatural discipline powers.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+disciplines|n           - List all your known disciplines and powers
  |y+power <name>|n          - Activate a discipline power
  |y+power <name>=<target>|n - Activate power on a target
  |y+powerinfo <name>|n      - Get detailed info on a specific power
  |y+effects|n               - View all active discipline effects on you

|WUSING POWERS:|n

Most discipline powers require a |yRouse Check|n:
  - Roll 1d10
  - |g6+:|n Success - no Hunger increase
  - |[R1-5:|n Failure - Hunger increases by 1

Some powers are |yFree|n (no Rouse check required).

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+disciplines|n
    List all your disciplines and available powers

  |y+power heightened senses|n
    Activate Auspex: Heightened Senses

  |y+power compel=Bob|n
    Use Dominate: Compel on Bob

  |y+powerinfo cloud memory|n
    Get detailed information about Cloud Memory power

  |y+effects|n
    View all active effects on you (duration, source, etc.)

|WPOWER LEVELS:|n

Discipline powers are organized by level (1-5):
  - |yLevel 1-2:|n Basic powers, lower costs
  - |yLevel 3-4:|n Advanced powers, significant effects
  - |yLevel 5:|n Master powers, dramatic effects

You must have the appropriate discipline rating to use powers:
  - Auspex 2 unlocks all Level 1-2 Auspex powers
  - Auspex 4 unlocks all Level 1-4 Auspex powers

|WAMALGAM POWERS:|n

Some powers require multiple disciplines:
  - Example: |yCloud Memory|n requires Dominate 3 + Obfuscate 1
  - You must have |yboth|n prerequisites to use amalgam powers

|WPOWER DURATION:|n

  - |yInstant:|n One-time effect, resolves immediately
  - |yScene:|n Lasts for current scene (typically one hour)
  - |yNight:|n Lasts until next sunrise
  - |yPermanent:|n Effect is permanent once activated

|WDISCIPLINE CATEGORIES:|n

  - |yPhysical:|n Celerity, Fortitude, Potence, Protean
  - |yMental:|n Auspex, Dominate, Obfuscate
  - |ySocial:|n Animalism, Presence
  - |ySupernatural:|n Blood Sorcery, Oblivion, Thin-Blood Alchemy

|WNOTES:|n

  - Using powers at Hunger 5 risks frenzy
  - Some powers require line of sight or physical touch
  - Dominate typically requires eye contact with mortals
  - Failed Rouse checks increase Hunger - manage carefully
