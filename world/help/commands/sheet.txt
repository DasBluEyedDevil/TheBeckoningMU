|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                       CHARACTER SHEET                             |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

View your complete character sheet or another character's public information.

|WUSAGE:|n

  |y+sheet|n              - View your own complete character sheet
  |y+sheet <character>|n  - View another character's sheet
  |y+st|n                 - View abbreviated status (quick reference)

|WDISPLAYED INFORMATION:|n

|yPersonal Info:|n
  - Name, Clan, Generation, Blood Potency
  - Predator Type, Sire, Coterie

|yVital Statistics:|n
  - Health, Willpower, Humanity
  - Blood Pool, Hunger level
  - Experience Points (current/total/unspent)

|yAttributes:|n Physical, Social, Mental (9 total)
|ySkills:|n Physical, Social, Mental (27 total)
|yDisciplines:|n Known disciplines with power levels
|yAdvantages:|n Backgrounds, Merits, Flaws
|ySocial:|n Status, Boons owed/owing

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+sheet|n
  |y+sheet Alice|n
  |y+st|n

|WNOTES:|n

  - Your own sheet shows all private information
  - Other characters' sheets show only public info
  - Use |y+st|n for a quick at-a-glance summary

|[r+===================================================================+|n

|ySee also:|n help chargen, help xp, help status, help disciplines
