|W|                    HUMANITY & STAIN COMMANDS                      |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Manage your character's Humanity, Convictions, and Stains.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+humanity|n                      - View Humanity, Convictions, Touchstones
  |y+humanity/conviction <text>|n    - Add a Conviction (max 3)
  |y+humanity/touchstone <text>|n    - Add a Touchstone (max Humanity ÷ 2)
  |y+stain|n                         - Add a Stain (Humanity violation)
  |y+stain <reason>|n                - Add Stain with reason
  |y+remorse|n                       - Attempt Remorse roll to remove Stains
  |y+frenzy|n                        - Check frenzy risk or resist frenzy

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+humanity|n
    View your Humanity rating, Convictions, Touchstones, and Stains

  |y+humanity/conviction I will not harm innocents|n
    Add "I will not harm innocents" as a Conviction

  |y+humanity/touchstone My sister Emily|n
    Add "My sister Emily" as a Touchstone

  |y+stain murdered an innocent mortal|n
    Add a Stain for violating your Convictions

  |y+remorse|n
    Attempt Remorse roll to clear Stains and possibly raise Humanity

  |y+frenzy|n
    Check your current frenzy status or attempt to resist frenzy

|WHUMANITY SYSTEM:|n

|yHumanity Rating:|n 0-10 scale
  - Represents connection to mortal life
  - Starts at 7 during chargen
  - Decreases with Stains
  - Affects frenzy resistance and Touchstone capacity

|yConvictions:|n Personal moral code (max 3)
  - Define what your character values
  - Violating Convictions causes Stains
  - Should be specific and personal
  - Examples: "Protect the innocent", "Never break my word"

|yTouchstones:|n Mortal connections (max = Humanity ÷ 2)
  - Represent ties to humanity
  - Help resist the Beast
  - Can be family, friends, or meaningful mortals
  - Losing Touchstones damages Humanity

|WSTAINS:|n

You gain Stains when you:
  - Violate your Convictions
  - Commit inhumane acts
  - Give in to the Beast
  - Break Humanity-related Tenets

|yStain Accumulation:|n
  - Minor violation: 1 Stain
  - Moderate violation: 2 Stains
  - Major violation: 3-4 Stains
  - Atrocity: 5+ Stains

When Stains ≥ Humanity: |[RHumanity check required|n

|WREMORSE ROLL:|n

At the end of a story/session with Stains:
1. Roll dice equal to |yHumanity rating|n
2. Success (6+): Remove all Stains
3. Failure: Lose 1 Humanity, Stains remain
4. Bestial Failure (multiple 1s): Lose 2 Humanity

|yIncreasing Humanity:|n
  - Requires 5+ successes on Remorse roll
  - Must have been acting virtuously
  - Costs XP (Current Rating × 10)

|WFRENZY:|n

The Beast within may force frenzy when:
  - At Hunger 4-5
  - Exposed to fire or sunlight
  - Provoked or humiliated
  - Threatened

Resist with: |yComposure + Humanity vs difficulty|n

|yFrenzy Types:|n
  - |[RFury Frenzy:|n Violent rage, attack everything
  - |[RHunger Frenzy:|n Uncontrollable feeding
  - |[RTerror Frenzy:|n Flee in panic (Rötschreck)

|WSTORYTELLERLESS CONSEQUENCES:|n

For solo RP or automated events:
  - Breaking Convictions triggers automatic Stains
  - Chronicle tracks patterns of behavior
  - Excessive Stains may trigger automated Humanity loss
  - Staff reviews significant Humanity changes

|WNOTES:|n

  - Convictions should reflect your character's personality
  - Touchstones provide roleplay opportunities
  - Humanity loss is serious - protect your morality
  - Low Humanity makes you more monstrous
  - Staff arbitrates edge cases and major violations
