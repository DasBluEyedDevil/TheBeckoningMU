|W|                       STATUS COMMANDS                             |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

View and manage Camarilla social status and positions.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+status|n                  - View your status and positions
  |y+status <character>|n      - View someone's status
  |y+positions|n               - List all positions and their holders
  |y+statusreq <position>/<reason>|n - Request a position
  |y+statusadmin|n             - Admin status management (staff only)

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+status|n
    View your own status rating and position

  |y+status Alice|n
    View Alice's public status

  |y+positions|n
    See all available positions and current holders

  |y+statusreq Sheriff/I have proven my loyalty and combat prowess|n
    Request to be appointed as Sheriff

|WSTATUS RATING:|n

Your total status is the sum of:
  - |yEarned Status:|n Personal reputation and accomplishments
  - |yPosition Status:|n Bonus from held position

Status ranges from |y0|n (fledgling) to |y5+|n (elder or titled)

|WCAMARILLA POSITIONS:|n

|yPrince|n - Ruler of the domain (Status +3)
  - Ultimate authority in the city
  - Maintains Masquerade and Traditions

|ySeneschal|n - Prince's advisor and second (Status +2)
  - Manages domain in Prince's absence
  - Handles administrative matters

|ySheriff|n - Keeper of the peace (Status +2)
  - Enforces Prince's will
  - Handles security and law enforcement

|yScourge|n - Warden of territory (Status +1)
  - Hunts intruders and thin-bloods
  - Territorial enforcement

|yKeeper of Elysium|n - Protector of neutral ground (Status +1)
  - Maintains sanctity of Elysium
  - Hosts gatherings and events

|yHarpy|n - Social arbiter (Status +1)
  - Tracks boons and favors
  - Officiates social disputes
  - Gossip and information broker

|yWhip|n - Clan representative (Status +1)
  - Represents clan interests to Prince
  - Maintains clan unity

|yPrimogen|n - Clan elder council (Status +2)
  - Advises Prince on domain matters
  - Represents clan in politics

|WREQUESTING A POSITION:|n

To request a position:
1. Have appropriate status and reputation
2. Use |y+statusreq <position>/<reason>|n
3. Prince or staff reviews your request
4. Appointment granted or denied

Factors considered:
  - IC reputation and accomplishments
  - Current status rating
  - Character competence
  - Political support
  - Domain needs

|WSTATUS BENEFITS:|n

Higher status provides:
  - |ySocial influence|n over lower-status Kindred
  - |yAccess|n to exclusive gatherings
  - |yRespect|n from other vampires
  - |yPolitical power|n in domain decisions
  - |yResource access|n through connections

|WLOSING STATUS:|n

Status can be lost through:
  - Violation of Traditions
  - Failure to fulfill duties
  - Political disgrace
  - Prince's disfavor
  - Major Masquerade breaches

|WNOTES:|n

  - Status is earned through RP and accomplishments
  - Positions come with responsibilities, not just benefits
  - Political intrigue is key to status games
  - The Prince can grant or strip status at will
  - Staff oversees position appointments
