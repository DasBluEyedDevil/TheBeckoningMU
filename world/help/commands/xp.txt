|W║                     EXPERIENCE POINTS                             |
|[R╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗|n

View and manage your character's experience points.

|WCOMMANDS:|n

  |y+xp|n                 - View XP balance and recent transactions
  |y+spend <trait>=<value>|n - Spend XP to improve a trait
  |y+xpaward <char>=<amount>/<reason>|n - Award XP (staff only)

|WXP COSTS:|n

|yAttributes:|n Current Rating × 5 XP
  - Example: Strength 2→3 costs 10 XP (2 × 5)

|ySkills:|n Current Rating × 3 XP
  - Example: Brawl 1→2 costs 3 XP (1 × 3)

|yIn-Clan Disciplines:|n Current Rating × 3 XP
  - Example: Celerity 2→3 costs 6 XP (2 × 3)

|yOut-of-Clan Disciplines:|n Current Rating × 5 XP
  - Example: Obfuscate 1→2 costs 5 XP (1 × 5)

|yHumanity:|n Current Rating × 10 XP
  - Example: Humanity 5→6 costs 50 XP (5 × 10)

|yWillpower:|n 3 XP per dot

|ySpecialties:|n 3 XP each

|WEXAMPLES:|n

  |y+xp|n
    View your XP balance and spending log

  |y+spend strength=3|n
    Increase Strength to 3 (costs current × 5 XP)

  |y+spend brawl=2|n
    Increase Brawl to 2 (costs current × 3 XP)

  |y+spend celerity=3|n
    Increase Celerity to 3 (in-clan: current × 3 XP)

  |y+xpaward Alice=5/excellent roleplay|n
    (Staff) Award 5 XP to Alice

|WEARNING XP:|n

  - |yWeekly Activity:|n 1-2 XP for active play
  - |yStorytellin g Goals:|n 1-3 XP for completing story arcs
  - |yExceptional RP:|n Bonus XP for outstanding roleplay
  - |yStaff Awards:|n Variable XP for significant contributions

|WNOTES:|n

  - You must meet prerequisites to raise traits
  - Some traits require IC justification
  - Discipline increases may require training time
  - Staff may approve exceptions to costs
