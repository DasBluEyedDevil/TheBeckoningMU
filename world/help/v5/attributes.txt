|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                         ATTRIBUTES                                |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Attributes represent your character's innate capabilities. All characters
have 9 attributes rated from 1 (poor) to 5 (exceptional).

|WPHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES:|n

|yStrength|n - Raw physical power and muscle
  - Lifting, pushing, breaking objects
  - Melee damage and grappling
  - Feats of physical might
  - Dice pools: Brawl, Melee, Athletics (for jumping/climbing)

|yDexterity|n - Agility, reflexes, and coordination
  - Dodging attacks (Defense = Dexterity + Athletics)
  - Fine manipulation and sleight of hand
  - Ranged combat accuracy
  - Dice pools: Firearms, Larceny, Stealth, Drive

|yStamina|n - Endurance and physical resilience
  - Determines Health (Stamina + 3)
  - Resisting fatigue and disease
  - Long-distance running
  - Surviving harsh conditions
  - Dice pools: Athletics (endurance), Survival

|WSOCIAL ATTRIBUTES:|n

|yCharisma|n - Force of personality and likability
  - First impressions and natural charm
  - Inspiring others
  - Leading by example
  - Dice pools: Performance, Leadership, Animal Ken

|yManipulation|n - Persuasiveness and cunning
  - Convincing others through argument
  - Lying and deception
  - Subtle influence
  - Dice pools: Persuasion, Subterfuge, Intimidation

|yComposure|n - Self-control and poise under pressure
  - Determines Willpower (Composure + Resolve)
  - Resisting frenzy and provocation
  - Maintaining calm in crisis
  - Firearms accuracy (steady hands)
  - Dice pools: Etiquette, Streetwise, Firearms

|WMENTAL ATTRIBUTES:|n

|yIntelligence|n - Reasoning and analytical thinking
  - Problem-solving and logic
  - Education and knowledge retention
  - Research and investigation
  - Dice pools: Academics, Science, Finance, Medicine

|yWits|n - Quick thinking and situational awareness
  - Initiative and reaction time
  - Noticing details
  - Improvisation
  - Dice pools: Awareness, Investigation, Insight

|yResolve|n - Determination and mental fortitude
  - Determines Willpower (Composure + Resolve)
  - Resisting mental influence (Dominate)
  - Maintaining focus under stress
  - Long-term dedication
  - Dice pools: Intimidation, Occult

|WCHARACTER CREATION:|n

During chargen, distribute attributes in priority:
  - |yPrimary:|n 7 dots (can raise up to 4 in one attribute)
  - |ySecondary:|n 5 dots
  - |yTertiary:|n 3 dots

All attributes start at |w1|n (minimum) by default.
Maximum during chargen: |w4|n
Maximum with XP: |w5|n

|WATTRIBUTE RATINGS:|n

  |y1|n - Poor: Below average human capability
  |y2|n - Average: Typical human level
  |y3|n - Good: Above average, competent
  |y4|n - Excellent: Among the best mortals
  |y5|n - Outstanding: Peak human performance

Vampires with 5 in an attribute are truly exceptional.

|WXP COSTS:|n

Raising attributes costs: |yCurrent Rating × 5 XP|n

Examples:
  - Strength 2→3: 10 XP (2 × 5)
  - Dexterity 3→4: 15 XP (3 × 5)
  - Intelligence 4→5: 20 XP (4 × 5)

|WDICE POOLS:|n

Most actions combine Attribute + Skill:
  - Physical action: Physical Attribute + Physical Skill
  - Social action: Social Attribute + Social Skill
  - Mental action: Mental Attribute + Mental Skill

Examples:
  - Attack: Strength + Brawl
  - Sneak: Dexterity + Stealth
  - Persuade: Manipulation + Persuasion
  - Investigate: Intelligence + Investigation

|WTIPS FOR ALLOCATION:|n

|yBalanced:|n Spread dots evenly (versatile but not exceptional)
|ySpecialized:|n Focus on 2-3 attributes (excellent at specific things)
|yMin-Max:|n Maximize one attribute, accept weaknesses elsewhere

Consider your character concept:
  - |yBrawler:|n High Strength, Dexterity, Stamina
  - |ySocialite:|n High Charisma, Manipulation, Composure
  - |yScholar:|n High Intelligence, Wits, Resolve

|WNOTES:|n

  - Attributes are harder to raise than Skills (cost more XP)
  - Choose wisely during chargen
  - Zero is |yimpossible|n - minimum is 1
  - Some supernatural powers may boost attributes temporarily
