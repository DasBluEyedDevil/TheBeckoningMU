|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                       VAMPIRE CLANS                               |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Each vampire belongs to a |yclan|n - a bloodline with distinct traits,
disciplines, and a supernatural |[Rbane|n.

|WCAMARILLA CLANS:|n

|yBrujah|n - The Rabble, passionate rebels and philosophers
  - Disciplines: |wCelerity, Potence, Presence|n
  - Bane: |[RViolent temper|n - frenzy tests at +2 difficulty
  - Compulsion: |[RRebellion|n - must resist authority

|yGangrel|n - The Outlanders, feral nomads and survivalists
  - Disciplines: |wAnimalism, Fortitude, Protean|n
  - Bane: |[RBestial features|n - animal traits appear when Hungry
  - Compulsion: |[RFeral Impulses|n - revert to animal instincts

|yMalkavian|n - The Lunatics, seers cursed with madness
  - Disciplines: |wAuspex, Dominate, Obfuscate|n
  - Bane: |[ROracle's Curse|n - plagued by visions and fractured insight
  - Compulsion: |[RDelusion|n - act on fractured perceptions

|yNosferatu|n - The Sewer Rats, hideously deformed information brokers
  - Disciplines: |wAnimalism, Obfuscate, Potence|n
  - Bane: |[RRepulsiveness|n - all social rolls at +2 difficulty
  - Compulsion: |[RCryptophilia|n - must hoard secrets

|yToreador|n - The Degenerates, artists and hedonists
  - Disciplines: |wAuspex, Celerity, Presence|n
  - Bane: |[RAesthetic Fixation|n - entranced by beauty
  - Compulsion: |[RObsession|n - fixate on beauty to exclusion of all else

|yTremere|n - The Warlocks, blood sorcerers and scholars
  - Disciplines: |wAuspex, Blood Sorcery, Dominate|n
  - Bane: |[RDeficient Blood|n - blood bonds form easier on them
  - Compulsion: |[RPerfectionism|n - cannot accept failure

|yVentrue|n - The Blue Bloods, nobles and leaders
  - Disciplines: |wDominate, Fortitude, Presence|n
  - Bane: |[RRarefied Taste|n - can only feed on specific type of victim
  - Compulsion: |[RArrogance|n - must assert dominance

|WANNARCHIST & INDEPENDENT CLANS:|n

|yBanu Haqim|n - The Assassins, judges and executioners
  - Disciplines: |wBlood Sorcery, Celerity, Obfuscate|n
  - Bane: |[RBlood Addiction|n - crave vampire blood
  - Compulsion: |[RJudgment|n - must punish wrongdoers

|yHecata|n - The Necromancers, death cultists (Giovanni, Harbingers, etc.)
  - Disciplines: |wAuspex, Fortitude, Oblivion|n
  - Bane: |[RPainful Kiss|n - bite causes agony, feeding is obvious
  - Compulsion: |[RMorbidity|n - obsess over death

|yLasombra|n - The Night Clan, shadow-wielding predators
  - Disciplines: |wDominate, Oblivion, Potence|n
  - Bane: |[RDistortion|n - don't appear in mirrors/recordings
  - Compulsion: |[RRuthlessness|n - must dominate and exploit

|yMinistry|n - The Setites, corrupters and tempters
  - Disciplines: |wObfuscate, Presence, Protean|n
  - Bane: |[RLight Sensitivity|n - penalty in bright light
  - Compulsion: |[RTransgression|n - must corrupt others

|yTzimisce|n - The Fiends, fleshcrafters and territorial lords
  - Disciplines: |wAnimalism, Dominate, Protean|n
  - Bane: |[RCohesion|n - must sleep near haven soil or take damage
  - Compulsion: |[RCovetousness|n - obsess over possessions

|yRavnos|n - The Charlatans, tricksters and wanderers
  - Disciplines: |wAnimalism, Obfuscate, Presence|n
  - Bane: |[RDoomed|n - tied to vice, frenzy when denied
  - Compulsion: |[RTemptation|n - must indulge vice

|WCHOOSING YOUR CLAN:|n

Your clan determines:
  - |yStarting disciplines|n (in-clan are cheaper)
  - |ySupernatural bane|n (permanent curse)
  - |yCompulsion|n (triggered by Bestial Failures)
  - |ySocial reputation|n among Kindred

Command: |y+chargen|n to begin character creation
