|W║                        PREDATOR TYPES                             |
|[R╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗|n

Your Predator Type defines how you hunt and feed. It reflects your
relationship with predation and the mortal world, granting specific
bonuses and penalties.

|WSELECTING A PREDATOR TYPE:|n

During chargen (Step 3), choose one Predator Type.
Each type provides:
  - |yBonus Skills:|n Free skill dots
  - |yBonus Disciplines:|n Free discipline dot (sometimes)
  - |yHunting Advantages:|n Bonuses to specific hunting locations
  - |yRoleplay Hooks:|n Defines your feeding style

|WPREDATOR TYPES:|n

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|yALLEYCAT|n - Hunt the homeless, desperate, and forgotten
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Streetwise
  - +1 Survival or Potence

|wHunting:|n
  - +2 dice on Street hunts
  - Prey: Homeless, addicts, runaways

|wConcept:|n
  You hunt in alleys and dark streets, preying on those society has
  abandoned. Quick, brutal, and efficient.

|wStrengths:|n Easy prey, low investigation risk
|wWeaknesses:|n Poor blood quality, dangerous areas

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|yBAGGER|n - Use blood bags and hospital access
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Streetwise or Larceny
  - +1 Medicine
  - +1 Blood Potency

|wHunting:|n
  - +2 dice at Hospital hunts
  - Access to blood bags

|wConcept:|n
  You steal blood bags from hospitals, blood banks, or ambulances.
  Clean, safe, but morally questionable.

|wStrengths:|n No direct feeding, Masquerade-safe
|wWeaknesses:|n Lower hunger reduction, requires access

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|yBLOOD LEECH|n - Feed on other vampires
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Brawl
  - +1 Stealth or Celerity
  - -1 Humanity (start at 6)

|wHunting:|n
  - Feed on other Kindred
  - Dangerous but powerful

|wConcept:|n
  You prey on other vampires, drinking their vitae. Amaranth (diablerie)
  may tempt you. Other vampires fear and hate you.

|wStrengths:|n Powerful blood, potential for growth
|wWeaknesses:|n Extreme danger, social pariah, Humanity loss

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|yCONSENSUALIST|n - Feed from willing vessels
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Persuasion
  - +1 Medicine or Insight
  - +1 Humanity (start at 8)

|wHunting:|n
  - Feed from consenting mortals
  - May maintain relationships

|wConcept:|n
  You only feed from willing participants - BDSM community, blood dolls,
  or those you've convinced of your needs.

|wStrengths:|n Masquerade-safe, ethical, relationship-based
|wWeaknesses:|n Time-consuming, limited prey pool

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|yFARMER|n - Feed from animals
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Animal Ken
  - +1 Survival
  - +1 Animalism

|wHunting:|n
  - +2 dice on Rural hunts
  - Feed from animals

|wConcept:|n
  You feed exclusively on animals. Rats, dogs, livestock. It's less
  satisfying but avoids hurting humans.

|wStrengths:|n Masquerade-safe, ethical, easy access
|wWeaknesses:|n Poor hunger reduction, social stigma

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|yOSMOSIS|n - Feed during sex and intimate contact
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Persuasion or Subterfuge
  - +1 Presence
  - +1 Merit: Beautiful (lowers difficulty 1 for seduction)

|wHunting:|n
  - Feed during sexual encounters
  - Use seduction and intimacy

|wConcept:|n
  You mix feeding with pleasure, taking blood during sex or intimate
  moments. Seductive and sensual predator.

|wStrengths:|n Enjoyable, consensual, relationship potential
|wWeaknesses:|n Requires seduction time, emotional entanglements

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|ySANDMAN|n - Feed from sleeping victims
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Stealth or Medicine
  - +1 Auspex

|wHunting:|n
  - +2 dice on Residential hunts
  - Break into homes while victims sleep

|wConcept:|n
  You're a night stalker, breaking into homes to feed on sleeping
  mortals. Silent, surgical, and terrifying.

|wStrengths:|n Clean feeding, minimal risk
|wWeaknesses:|n Breaking and entering, potential awakening

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|ySCENE QUEEN|n - Feed at clubs, parties, and events
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Persuasion or Streetwise
  - +1 Performance or Subterfuge
  - +1 Presence or Potence

|wHunting:|n
  - +2 dice at Club hunts
  - Prey: Clubgoers, ravers, party animals

|wConcept:|n
  You haunt the nightclub scene, feeding on intoxicated partygoers.
  The music, crowds, and darkness provide perfect cover.

|wStrengths:|n Easy prey, social cover, fun atmosphere
|wWeaknesses:|n Witnesses, poor blood quality (drugs/alcohol)

|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n
|ySIREN|n - Seduce and entrap your prey
|[R+-------------------------------------------------------------------+|n

|wBonuses:|n
  - +1 Persuasion or Subterfuge
  - +1 Presence
  - +1 Merit: Looks (lowers difficulty 1 for seduction)

|wHunting:|n
  - +2 dice on Secured area hunts
  - Use seduction and manipulation

|wConcept:|n
  You're a master seducer, using supernatural charm to lure victims.
  You target the wealthy and powerful.

|wStrengths:|n High-quality prey, access to resources
|wWeaknesses:|n Time investment, risk of attachment

|WCHANGING PREDATOR TYPE:|n

Predator Type is generally |yfixed|n after chargen. Changing it requires:
  - Significant IC justification
  - Staff approval
  - May involve XP cost for retraining
  - Reflects major character development

|WROLEPLAY IMPLICATIONS:|n

Your Predator Type should influence:
  - How you describe feeding scenes
  - Your relationship with mortals
  - Your Humanity and morality
  - Your nightly activities
  - Your social connections

|WNOTES:|n

  - Predator Type is more than mechanics - it's character definition
  - Affects how you interact with the mortal world
  - Choose one that matches your concept and playstyle
  - Staff may require you to roleplay your chosen type
