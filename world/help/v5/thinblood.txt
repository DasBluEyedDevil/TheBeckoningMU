|[R+===================================================================+
|W|                        THIN-BLOOD VAMPIRES                        |
|[R+===================================================================+|n

Thin-Bloods are vampires with such diluted vitae that they barely
qualify as Kindred. Often called "Duskborn," they are outcasts in
vampire society but possess unique abilities.

|WTHIN-BLOOD TRAITS:|n

|yBlood Potency 0:|n
  - Cannot create Blood Bonds or ghouls
  - Weak vitae, often rejected by other vampires
  - Cannot sire childer

|ySunlight Tolerance:|n
  - Takes |ybashing|n damage from sunlight (not aggravated)
  - Can survive daylight hours, though painfully
  - Damage: 2 bashing per turn of exposure

|yBlush of Life:|n
  - Easier to maintain mortal appearance
  - Can sometimes pass as human at low Hunger
  - Automatic at Hunger 1-2

|yDisciplines:|n
  - Choose ONE discipline with a Thin-Blood flaw
  - OR learn |yThin-Blood Alchemy|n instead
  - Cannot learn disciplines normally (no Blood Potency)

|WTHIN-BLOOD ALCHEMY:|n

Unique supernatural ability to craft alchemical formulae:
  - Craft formulae using vampire blood + ingredients
  - No Rouse checks (Blood Potency 0)
  - Single-use concoctions
  - Effects similar to discipline powers

|yKnown Formulae:|n
  - |wFar Reach|n: Telekinesis (Level 1)
  - |wHaze|n: Cloud minds (Level 1)
  - |wEnvelop|n: Shadow concealment (Level 1)
  - |wCounterfeit|n: Duplicate objects (Level 2)
  - |wDefractionate|n: Split consciousness (Level 2)
  - Higher level formulae available with experience

|WSOCIAL STANDING:|n

Thin-Bloods are |rpariahs|n in vampire society:
  - Not recognized as true Kindred by most elders
  - Often hunted or enslaved
  - Subject to "Thin-Blood Pogroms" in some domains
  - Must prove worth to gain any standing

|WADVANTAGES:|n
  - Can survive daylight (unique among vampires)
  - Harder to detect as vampire
  - Alchemy provides versatile powers
  - Not bound by Blood Bonds

|WDISADVANTAGES:|n
  - Extremely weak vitae
  - Social pariah status
  - Cannot embrace, bond, or create ghouls
  - Vulnerable to other vampires

|[r+===================================================================+|n

|yCommands:|n
  |w+alchemy|n          - View and use Thin-Blood Alchemy
  |w+daylight|n         - Check sunlight tolerance
  |w+sheet|n            - View character (shows Thin-Blood status)

|ySee also:|n help alchemy, help chargen, help clans
